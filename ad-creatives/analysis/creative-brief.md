## Campaign Architecture

### Batch Size & Format Mix

**Total ad variations:** 15 concepts

| Format | Count | Percentage | Funnel Stage Focus |
|--------|-------|------------|-------------------|
| Video scripts (Reels/UGC) | 8 | 53% | TOF (5), MOF (2), BOF (1) |
| Carousel | 4 | 27% | MOF (1), BOF (3) |
| Static image | 3 | 20% | BOF (2), TOF (1) |

**Rationale:** Video dominates because UGC Reels get 4x the CTR of brand creative and 3.2% engagement vs 1.1% for static. Carousel is second because it has the highest ROAS and lowest CPA. Static serves retargeting and direct-offer roles only.

### Funnel Stage Distribution

| Stage | Count | Purpose | Primary CTA |
|-------|-------|---------|-------------|
| TOF (Cold) | 6 | Awareness, hook testing, audience building | "Comment RESET" / "Take the free quiz" |
| MOF (Warm) | 4 | Education, mechanism, objection handling | "Learn more" / "Take the quiz" |
| BOF (Hot) | 5 | Conversion, retargeting, social proof | "Tap the link -- it's $27" |

### Testing Framework

**Wave 1 (Launch First -- Week 1-2):**
- Deploy 6 ads (Concepts 1, 2, 3, 7, 10, 13)
- Spend $20-$30/day across all 6
- Kill any ad with CTR below 0.3% after 3 days and $15+ spend
- Scale winners to $50-$100/day

**Wave 2 (Follow-Up -- Week 3-4):**
- Deploy remaining 9 ads
- Use Wave 1 data to inform which hooks/formats to emphasize
- Build retargeting audiences from Wave 1 video viewers (25%+ watched) and link clickers

**Ongoing Creative Testing Rules:**
- Test ONE variable at a time (hook, visual, CTA -- not all three)
- Need 1,000+ impressions before judging an ad
- Winning metric: CPA (cost per $27 purchase), not CTR alone
- Refresh creative every 3-4 weeks to prevent fatigue

## Creative Mandatories

### Brand Voice Rules
- **Always validate first.** Every ad must acknowledge the struggle before offering a solution.
- **Warm, direct, nurse-to-nurse.** Like a colleague who figured something out and is sharing it.
- **Science-backed in plain language.** Explain cortisol and circadian rhythm simply. No jargon.
- **Never shame.** No "just eat less, move more" energy. No hustle-culture messaging.
- **Specific to shift work.** Reference 12-hour shifts, night shifts, rotating schedules, post-shift exhaustion.

### Visual Identity
- **Video ads:** Raw, phone-quality, natural lighting. NOT branded/polished. Should look like a nurse filmed it.
- **Carousel and static ads:** Gold, cream, and elegant -- SmithFit brand aesthetic. Clean typography. High contrast.
- **IJ in video:** Scrubs or casual. Badge visible when possible. Eye contact with camera.
- **B-roll:** Nurse-life visuals (scrubs, hospital parking lot, coffee, night shift, badge lanyard).
- **Text overlays:** Bold, 2-5 words, restating the hook. White or gold text on slightly dimmed background.
- **Captions:** Always on. 85%+ of viewers watch without sound.

### Required Proof Points (Rotate Across Ads)
- "400+ nurses have done this" (volume social proof)
- "87% feel a difference by Day 3" (speed + specificity)
- "Average 5 lbs in Week 1" (result -- use only in testimonial context with disclaimer)
- "60-day money-back guarantee" (risk reversal)
- "$27" (price transparency)
- "Built by a nurse, for nurses" (authority + identity)
- IJ's RN credential (shown or stated)
- Sarah's story (38, 3 kids + adopting 4th, eating more, lost 30 lbs in 8.5 weeks -- first-person testimonial only)

### Prohibited Language & Content
- "Boost metabolism" / "Balance hormones" / "Cleanse toxins" (Meta rejects these)
- "Lose X lbs in Y days" as a general claim (only allowed in first-person testimonial with "results may vary")
- "Guaranteed results" / "100% effective" / "Most effective"
- Before-and-after side-by-side images (Meta-prohibited)
- "Lazy" / "Discipline" / "Just do it" / any shame-based language
- Generic fitness advice that could apply to anyone (every ad must be nurse/shift-specific)
- Body-inadequacy language ("fix your body," "your body is wrong")
- Countdown timers or fake scarcity ("only 5 spots left" unless actually true)

### CTA Destinations
- **Primary (BOF):** $27 course purchase page (direct link)
- **Secondary (TOF/MOF):** Lead magnet quiz (lead-magnet-quiz.html -- once deployed with webhook)
- **Organic/Engagement:** "Comment RESET" (DM automation sends link)
- **All ads:** Include "results may vary" and "not medical advice" in ad text or landing page

## Compliance Checklist (Pre-Launch)

Before any ad goes live, verify:

- [ ] No before-and-after side-by-side images
- [ ] No specific weight loss claims outside first-person testimonials
- [ ] All testimonials include "results may vary" (text overlay or ad copy)
- [ ] No "boost metabolism," "balance hormones," or "cleanse toxins" language
- [ ] No guaranteed timelines ("results in 24 hours")
- [ ] No body-inadequacy language
- [ ] No superlatives ("most effective," "strongest")
- [ ] "Not medical advice" disclaimer on landing page
- [ ] CTA destination (purchase page or quiz) is functional and mobile-optimized
- [ ] All client testimonials have written permission for ad use
- [ ] Lead magnet quiz webhook and purchase link URLs are configured (for Concept 13)

## Production Requirements Summary

### IJ Filming Session (Covers Concepts 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 7, 8)
- **Setup:** Phone on tripod, natural light, simple background OR relevant setting (home, outside hospital)
- **Wardrobe options:** Scrubs (Concepts 1, 3, 7, 8) and casual clothes (Concepts 2, 4)
- **Format:** Vertical (9:16), 1080x1920 minimum
- **Audio:** Quiet room, no background noise. External lapel mic recommended but not required.
- **Deliverable:** Film ALL 7 scripts in one session. Multiple takes of each. Raw footage to editor for captions, text overlays, and cuts.
- **Estimated time:** 60-90 minutes for all 7 scripts

### Client UGC Collection (Covers Concepts 6, 9)
- **Outreach:** Send filming instructions to 3-5 clients with strong results
- **Instructions to provide:** Film yourself on your phone. Vertical format. Natural lighting. Follow the script direction loosely -- your own words are better than scripted. 15-30 seconds. Send the raw video to [submission method].
- **Permission:** Written consent for ad use required before publishing

### Graphic Design (Covers Concepts 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15)
- **Brand template:** Gold/cream palette, clean typography, SmithFit logo small in corner
- **Tool:** Canva, Figma, or designer -- use SmithFit brand kit
- **Deliverables:** Each concept as multiple sizes (1080x1080 for feed, 1080x1920 for stories, 1200x628 for Facebook feed)
