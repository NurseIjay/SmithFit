**Campaign:** Paid Media Launch
**Platforms:** Meta (Facebook + Instagram)
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-26

---

## Universal Settings (Apply to All Ad Sets)

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Age | 35-55 |
| Gender | Women |
| Locations | United States (all states) |
| Language | English |
| Placements | Automatic (let Meta optimize), but review performance by placement weekly |

### Universal Exclusions (Apply to All Ad Sets)
- Current customers (upload customer email list as Custom Audience, exclude)
- People who purchased in the last 30 days
- SmithFit team members / employees
- Men (this is a women-focused program -- IJ coaches women, not men)

## Core Audience 1: Nurse Interest Stack

**Purpose:** Reach nurses directly through interest-based targeting. This is the primary cold traffic audience.

**Targeting:**

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Age | 35-55 |
| Gender | Women |
| Interests (include ALL -- OR logic) | Nursing, Registered nurse, Licensed practical nurse, Certified nursing assistant, Respiratory therapist, Nurse practitioner, Travel nursing, Night shift, American Nurses Association, Scrubs (clothing) |
| Narrow audience (AND) | Weight loss OR Healthy eating OR Fitness and wellness OR Metabolism |
| Exclusions | Current customers, recent purchasers |

**Estimated audience size:** 2-5 million (will vary by Meta's current data)

**Best ads for this audience:** 01 (Ghost in Scrubs), 02 (You're Not Lazy), 03 (Night Shift Nurses), 04 (Coffee and Vibes), 05 (3 Things), 13 (Quiz)

**Budget allocation:** 40% of total ad spend

**Estimated audience size:** 3-8 million

**Best ads for this audience:** 02 (You're Not Lazy), 03 (Night Shift Nurses), 05 (3 Things), 07 (Why Diet Fails), 13 (Quiz)

## Core Audience 3: Health/Wellness Broad (Scale Audience)

**Purpose:** Broader reach for scaling winning ads. Let Meta's algorithm find the right people within a wider pool. Use ONLY with proven winning creative.

**Targeting:**

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Age | 35-55 |
| Gender | Women |
| Interests | Women's health OR Metabolism OR Hormonal health OR Cortisol OR Menopause OR Perimenopause |
| Narrow audience (AND) | Fitness OR Weight loss OR Healthy lifestyle |
| Exclusions | Current customers, recent purchasers |

**Estimated audience size:** 10-20 million

**Best ads for this audience:** Only deploy PROVEN winners here. Start with the top 2-3 ads from Core Audience 1 testing.

**Budget allocation:** 10% initially, scale to 25% with proven creative

**Note:** This audience is intentionally broader. The ad creative itself acts as the targeting filter -- nurse-specific language in the ad naturally filters for the right person, even in a broad audience. This is how Advantage+ campaigns work.

## Retargeting: Warm Audience

**Purpose:** Re-engage people who showed interest but didn't purchase. These people know who SmithFit is.

**Source audiences (Custom Audiences):**

| Source | Window | Estimated Size |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Video viewers (25%+ watched any SmithFit video ad) | Last 30 days | Builds over time |
| Instagram profile visitors | Last 30 days | Builds over time |
| Instagram post/ad engagers | Last 60 days | Builds over time |
| Facebook page engagers | Last 60 days | Builds over time |
| Quiz starters who didn't complete | Last 30 days | Builds over time |

**Combined into one Custom Audience:** "Warm -- Engaged but Not Purchased"

**Exclusions:** Current customers, purchasers, anyone in Hot Retargeting audience

**Best ads for this audience:** 07 (Why Diet Fails), 08 (Objection Buster), 11 (Not a Diet), 12 (Feel Like Yourself), 14 (Community)

**Budget allocation:** 15% of total ad spend

**Frequency cap:** 3 impressions per person per week (avoid ad fatigue)

## Retargeting: Hot Audience

**Purpose:** Final conversion push for people who visited the purchase page or quiz but didn't buy. These are the most purchase-ready people.

**Source audiences (Custom Audiences):**

| Source | Window | Estimated Size |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Purchase page visitors (didn't purchase) | Last 14 days | Builds over time |
| Quiz completers (got result, didn't purchase) | Last 14 days | Builds over time |
| Add-to-cart / checkout initiated (didn't complete) | Last 7 days | Builds over time |
| Video viewers (75%+ watched any SmithFit video ad) | Last 14 days | Builds over time |

**Combined into one Custom Audience:** "Hot -- High Intent, Not Converted"

**Exclusions:** Current customers, purchasers

**Best ads for this audience:** 09 (Still Thinking About It), 10 (What Nurses Are Saying), 15 (DoorDash Reframe)

**Budget allocation:** 10% of total ad spend

**Frequency cap:** 5 impressions per person per week (higher frequency is acceptable here -- they're close to buying)

## Platform-Specific Notes

### Instagram
- Schedule Reels for 10 PM-7 AM to catch night-shift nurses scrolling on break
- Reels algorithm favors saves and shares -- educational content (Ad 05, 07) earns these
- "Comment RESET" CTAs boost engagement signals, improving organic reach
- Stories ads are ideal for retargeting (short, direct, frequent)

### Facebook
- Advantage+ campaigns perform well with diverse creative -- feed Meta 3-5 ad variations and let the algorithm optimize
- Carousel format has lowest CPA on Facebook specifically -- prioritize Ad 10 and 14 here
- Upload customer email list for exclusion AND for Lookalike source
- Facebook Groups content is being boosted -- SmithFit community angle (Ad 14) plays into this trend

### Cross-Platform
- Run the SAME ad on both platforms initially. Let Meta optimize placement.
- After 7 days, check placement breakdown. If one platform dramatically outperforms, create platform-specific ad sets.
- Video (Reels) for TOF awareness on both platforms
- Carousel for MOF/BOF conversion on Facebook
- Stories for retargeting on Instagram

## Audience Build Checklist (Pre-Launch)

- [ ] Upload customer email list to Meta as Custom Audience (for exclusion and Lookalike source)
- [ ] Create "Warm -- Engaged but Not Purchased" Custom Audience (video viewers + engagers)
- [ ] Create "Hot -- High Intent, Not Converted" Custom Audience (page visitors + quiz completers)
- [ ] Install Facebook Pixel on: purchase page, quiz page, thank-you page
- [ ] Set up Custom Conversions: "Purchase Complete" and "Quiz Complete" events
- [ ] Verify Pixel is firing correctly (use Meta Pixel Helper Chrome extension)
- [ ] Create at least Core Audience 1 and Core Audience 2 ad sets before launch
