## THE HOOK (0-3 seconds)

**On-screen text:** "Night shift nurses -- stop blaming yourself."

**IJ says (voiceover):** "Night shift nurses. Stop blaming yourself."

**Visual:** B-roll montage -- hospital parking lot at night, or scrubs laid out, or a coffee cup next to a badge lanyard. Dark, moody lighting. These visuals scream "night shift" before a single word registers.

**Sound:** IJ's voice only over B-roll. No music.

---

## THE PROBLEM (3-10 seconds)

**IJ says (voiceover, then cut to on-camera at ~5 sec):** "Every diet you've tried was designed for people who sleep at night and eat at normal hours. That's not your life."

**Visual:** Cut from B-roll to IJ on camera at the 5-second mark. He's in scrubs. Direct eye contact.

**On-screen text:** "Diets aren't built for your schedule."

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Setting:** Two settings needed.
1. B-roll (0-5 sec): Film at night or in low light. Options: parking lot exterior, kitchen at odd hours, scrubs hanging on a door, coffee mug with a stethoscope nearby. IJ can film this on his phone -- it should look real, not staged.
2. Talking head (5-30 sec): Simple indoor background. Same setup as other video ads.

**Wardrobe:** Scrubs for the talking-head portion. B-roll shows scrubs as props/setting.

**Energy level:** Starts strong and direct ("stop blaming yourself" is a command). Shifts to empathetic explanation. Ends with quiet confidence.

**Camera:** Phone, vertical (9:16). B-roll can be handheld for rawness. Talking head on tripod.

**Editing notes:**
- B-roll clips: 1-2 seconds each, quick cuts. Hospital parking lot at night, coffee, scrubs, badge -- any 2-3 of these.
- Cut to IJ on camera at exactly the 5-second mark.
- Auto-captions throughout.
- Text overlays bold, positioned top-third.
- No music. The silence of the night-shift B-roll is the mood.
