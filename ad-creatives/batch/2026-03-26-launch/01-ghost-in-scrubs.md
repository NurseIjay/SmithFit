## THE HOOK (0-3 seconds)

**On-screen text:** "I'm an RN. And I felt like a ghost."

**IJ says:** "Two years ago, I was working 12-hour shifts and I felt like a ghost in my own body."

**Visual:** IJ in scrubs or casual clothes, speaking directly to camera. Close-up framing (chest up). Natural lighting. Phone-quality -- NOT polished. Badge lanyard visible if wearing scrubs.

**Sound:** IJ's voice only. No music. Raw and real.

---

## THE PROBLEM (3-8 seconds)

**IJ says:** "I'd tried everything. Keto. Intermittent fasting. More cardio. Nothing stuck."

**Visual:** Same talking-head shot. IJ's expression shifts from open to slightly frustrated as he lists the failures.

**On-screen text:** "Tried keto. IF. Cardio. Nothing."

---

## THE MECHANISM (8-20 seconds)

**IJ says:** "Because I was solving the wrong problem. It wasn't about eating less. It was about regulating my cortisol first. When you work 12-hour shifts, your body stays in fight-or-flight. And when cortisol stays elevated, your body holds onto fat no matter what you do. Once I figured that out, everything changed."

**Visual:** Same talking-head. IJ leans slightly forward at "solving the wrong problem" -- the energy shifts from frustrated to confident. This is the moment of realization.

**On-screen text (timed):**
- At "solving the wrong problem": "Wrong problem."
- At "regulating my cortisol first": "Regulate cortisol FIRST."

**Transition:** None. Single continuous shot. The rawness IS the production value.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Setting:** Home, simple background (clean wall, bookshelf, or standing outside). NOT a studio. NOT a gym.

**Wardrobe:** Option A: Scrubs (strongest identity signal). Option B: Casual clothes (relatable, off-duty feel). Film both -- test which performs better.

**Energy level:** Starts reflective and honest (ghost story). Builds to confident and warm (the solution). Ends invitational, not salesy.

**Camera:** Phone on tripod. Vertical (9:16). 1080x1920 minimum. IJ's eyes at camera lens level.

**Editing notes:**
- Add auto-captions (white text, dark background bar). 85%+ of viewers watch without sound.
- Text overlays in bold white or gold, positioned top-third of frame.
- No transitions, no B-roll, no music. This is a man telling his story. Keep it raw.
- Trim dead air at the start -- hook must land in first second.
