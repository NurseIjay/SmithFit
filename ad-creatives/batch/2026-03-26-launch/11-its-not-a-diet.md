## VISUAL CONCEPT

**Layout:** Centered text hierarchy on clean background. No photos. Typography-driven. The simplicity is the pattern interrupt -- it stands out in a feed full of busy fitness graphics.

**Background:** Cream (#FFF8F0) or warm off-white. Subtle texture optional (linen or paper grain).

**Main headline (large, bold, centered):**
"This is NOT another diet."

**Color:** Dark charcoal (#2D2D2D) or deep gold (#8B7535).

**Supporting text (medium, below headline, elegant):**
"It's a shift-proof metabolism reset built by a nurse, for nurses."

**Color:** Muted charcoal or medium gold.

**Proof line (smaller, below, with visual separator):**
"400+ nurses | $27 | 60-day guarantee"

**CTA (bottom, button-style or bold text):**
"Start the 7-Day Reset"

**SmithFit logo:** Small, bottom corner. Subtle -- not the focus.

**Color palette:**
- Background: Cream/warm white
- Primary text: Dark charcoal or deep gold
- Accent: Gold line separator between text blocks
- CTA area: Gold background with white text (or reverse)

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Design tool:** Canva or Figma.

**Sizes needed:**
- 1080x1080 (feed)
- 1080x1920 (Stories)
- 1200x628 (Facebook feed landscape option)

**Design principles:**
- White space is your friend. This ad should breathe.
- No photos, no illustrations. Text-only. The anti-diet message is reinforced by the anti-busy design.
- Typography hierarchy must be clear: headline > supporting text > proof line > CTA.
- The word "NOT" should be visually emphasized -- bold, larger, or different color from the rest of the headline.
