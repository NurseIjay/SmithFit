# SmithFit Ad Testing Plan
**Campaign:** Paid Media Launch
**Start Date:** TBD (when IJ films video batch and quiz is configured)
**Total Testing Budget:** $560-$840 over 4 weeks
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-26

---

## Testing Philosophy

Test ONE variable at a time. When testing hooks, keep the body and CTA the same. When testing audiences, keep the creative the same. When testing offers, keep everything else the same. Patience pays -- bad data from impatient testing costs more than the budget you think you're saving.

---

## Wave 1: Hook Testing (Week 1-2)

**Objective:** Find the top 2-3 hooks that stop the scroll and earn clicks.

**Budget:** $20-$30/day ($140-$210/week)

**Duration:** 7-10 days before making decisions

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Target (Video) | Target (Carousel) | Target (Static) |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| CTR (link click-through rate) | Above 0.5% | Above 0.4% | Above 0.3% |
| Engagement rate | Above 3% | Above 2% | Above 1.5% |
| Video completion (50%+) | Above 40% | -- | -- |
| Cost per link click | Below $2.00 | Below $2.50 | Below $3.00 |
| CPA ($27 purchase) | Below $30 | Below $30 | Below $25 |
| CPL (quiz lead, Ad 13 only) | -- | -- | Below $5 |

### Kill Criteria (When to Turn Off an Ad)

| Condition | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| CTR below 0.3% after $15+ spend | Kill |
| Zero link clicks after $10+ spend | Kill |
| CPA above $40 after $50+ spend | Kill |
| Engagement below 1% after 1,000+ impressions | Kill |
| Relevance score below 4/10 | Kill |

### Scale Criteria (When to Increase Budget)

| Condition | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| CPA below $20 after $30+ spend | Increase daily budget by 20% |
| CTR above 1% consistently for 3+ days | Increase daily budget by 30% |
| ROAS above 1.0 (breakeven or better on $27) | Scale to $50-$100/day |

### A/B Testing Rules for Wave 2

1. **Hook A/B tests:** Take the winning ad from Wave 1 and run its A/B variation hooks against the original. Each hook gets $15-$20 before judging.
2. **Audience A/B tests:** Run the same winning ad in Core Audience 1 vs. Core Audience 2 vs. Broad (Core 3). Same budget per audience.
3. **CTA A/B tests:** Test "Comment RESET" vs. "Link in bio" vs. "Tap below" on the same ad. One variable at a time.

## Wave 3: Scale Winners (Week 5+)

**Objective:** Pour budget into proven winners. Build retargeting audiences. Introduce Lookalikes.

**Budget:** $100-$300/day (scale based on ROAS)

### Scaling Rules

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Ad with CPA below $20 for 5+ consecutive days | Scale budget 20-30% every 3 days |
| Ad with CPA below $15 | This is a winner. Scale aggressively to $100+/day. |
| CPA creeps above $25 after scaling | Pause scaling. Check frequency (ad fatigue). Consider refreshing creative. |
| Frequency above 3.0 on cold audiences | Creative fatigue. Pause and create a new variation of the winning hook. |
| Frequency above 5.0 on retargeting | Rotate in new retargeting creative. |

### Scaling Milestones

| Milestone | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| 50 purchases | Upload purchaser list for Lookalike Audience source |
| 100 purchases | Create 1% Lookalike Audience. Start testing TOF winners against Lookalike. |
| 500 quiz completions | Create Quiz Completer Lookalike. Test alongside Purchaser Lookalike. |
| 1,000 video viewers (50%+) | Create Video Engager Lookalike for TOF expansion. |
| $1,000 total ad spend | Full audit: which ads, audiences, and placements are performing? Cut everything below breakeven. |

### Creative Refresh Schedule

| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| Week 4 | Create 2-3 new hook variations of top performers |
| Week 6 | Film new IJ video batch with fresh hooks (based on Wave 1-2 learnings) |
| Week 8 | Collect 2-3 new client UGC testimonials |
| Week 10 | Refresh carousel testimonials with new quotes |
| Monthly | Replace bottom-performing ads with new concepts |

## Budget Scaling Rules

### Conservative Path (Recommended for First Campaign)

| Total Weekly Spend | Trigger to Next Level |
|-------------------|-----------------------|
| $140-$210/week (Wave 1) | Default starting point |
| $300-$400/week (Wave 1 + 2) | After identifying 2+ winning ads |
| $500-$700/week (Scaling) | After 3+ consecutive days of CPA below $25 |
| $700-$1,400/week (Full scale) | After 2+ weeks of profitable ROAS |
| $1,400+/week (Aggressive scale) | After proven funnel economics (upsell revenue covers ad spend) |

### Emergency Rules

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Zero purchases after $200 total spend | Pause all ads. Audit: is the purchase page working? Is the pixel firing? Are the right audiences selected? |
| CPA above $35 across all ads for 7+ days | Reduce budget to minimum ($10/day). Test new hooks. Consider the offer or landing page is the issue, not the ad. |
| One ad spending 80%+ of budget with no conversions | Check audience overlap. Meta may be over-optimizing for the wrong audience. Split into separate ad sets. |
