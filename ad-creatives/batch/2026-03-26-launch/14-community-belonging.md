## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Design tool:** Canva or Figma.

**Sizes:** 1080x1080 per slide.

**Asset dependencies:** This carousel is strongest with real community screenshots -- coaching call screenshots, Win Wednesday celebration posts, member chat messages. These require:
1. Permission from members shown in screenshots
2. Names/faces blurred or only first names shown
3. Gold/cream overlay or border to match brand aesthetic

**If screenshots are not yet available:** Use text-only slides with the quotes and captions. The community FEELING can be conveyed through language even without visuals. Add screenshots later as a refresh.

**Design principles:**
- Warm, inviting, inclusive. This is about BELONGING, not features.
- Each slide should feel like a window into the community -- not a marketing pitch.
- Gold/cream aesthetic throughout for brand consistency.
- Quotes should be large and readable on mobile.
