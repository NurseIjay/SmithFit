## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Design tool:** Canva or Figma.

**Sizes needed:**
- Static: 1080x1080 (feed), 1080x1920 (Stories), 1200x628 (Facebook)
- Carousel: 1080x1080 per slide

**Design principles:**
- This ad should feel like a QUIZ, not a sales pitch. Clean, educational, curiosity-driven.
- The 4 stress types create intrigue -- "which am I?" is the psychological driver.
- "No purchase required" and "free" should be prominently visible. This lowers the barrier to zero.
- Gold/cream brand aesthetic applies.

**Deployment dependency:** The lead-magnet-quiz.html file must have its webhook URL and purchase link URL configured in GHL before this ad goes live. Without that, quiz completions won't capture emails or direct to the $27 offer.

**Funnel flow:**
1. User clicks ad -> lands on quiz
2. Completes quiz (2 min) -> email capture gate
3. Gets stress type result -> sees $27 course CTA
4. If no purchase -> enters Campaign 05 email nurture sequence
5. If purchase -> enters Campaign 01 or 02 depending on upsell path
