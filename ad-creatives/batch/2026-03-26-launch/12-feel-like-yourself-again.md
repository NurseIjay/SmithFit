## VISUAL CONCEPT

**Layout:** Centered text on warm, inviting background. This ad speaks to the FEELING, not the feature. Every design choice should feel warm, calm, and aspirational.

**Background:** Warm gold gradient (soft transition from light gold to cream) or solid warm cream with a subtle gold glow.

**Main headline (large, elegant):**
"Feel like yourself again."

**Font:** Serif or elegant sans-serif. This headline should feel like a promise whispered, not shouted.

**Color:** Dark charcoal or deep warm brown.

**Supporting text (medium, below):**
"The 7-Day Shift-Proof Metabolism Reset for nurses over 35."

**Detail line (smaller, gentle):**
"$27 | Built by an RN | 60-day guarantee"

**Aspirational line (smallest, bottom, italic or light weight):**
"Scrubs fitting. Energy back. Not sucking in your stomach."

**CTA:** "Start today"

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Design tool:** Canva or Figma.

**Sizes needed:**
- 1080x1080 (feed)
- 1080x1920 (Stories)
- 1200x628 (Facebook feed)

**Design principles:**
- This is the most emotionally-driven ad in the batch. The design should match -- warm, calm, inviting.
- No bold/aggressive typography. No urgency markers. The quiet confidence IS the selling.
- Gold/cream palette at its warmest here. Think: the feeling of putting on your favorite outfit and it fitting perfectly.
- Typography hierarchy: headline dominates, everything else supports.
- White space matters. Don't crowd the frame.

**Why this works:** Research from Blink Fitness ("Mood Above Muscle") and Peloton confirms that emotional/identity messaging outperforms outcome-based messaging for women. "Feel like yourself again" is not a weight-loss claim -- it's an identity promise. Fully Meta-compliant. And it hits the avatar's deepest desire: not to be thinner, but to feel like HER again.
