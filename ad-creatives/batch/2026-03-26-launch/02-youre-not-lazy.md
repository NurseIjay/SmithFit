## THE HOOK (0-3 seconds)

**On-screen text:** "You're not lazy."

**IJ says:** "You're not lazy."

**Visual:** IJ in scrubs, looking directly at camera. Serious, warm expression. Close framing. The two-word hook is both the verbal and visual opening -- they hit simultaneously.

**Sound:** IJ's voice only. No music. The pause after "You're not lazy" is intentional -- let it breathe for half a second.

---

## THE PROBLEM (3-8 seconds)

**IJ says:** "You're not broken. Your cortisol has been in overdrive for years."

**Visual:** Same shot. IJ's expression softens -- this is validation, not a lecture.

**On-screen text:** "Your cortisol is in overdrive."

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Setting:** Home or simple indoor background. Clean, uncluttered.

**Wardrobe:** Scrubs. The scrubs reinforce the RN credential without saying it. Badge visible if possible.

**Energy level:** Starts serious and validating. Builds conviction through the mechanism section. Ends calm and confident. This is NOT hype energy -- it's a colleague sharing something important.

**Camera:** Phone on tripod. Vertical (9:16). Eye-level. IJ centered in frame.

**Editing notes:**
- Auto-captions (white text, dark background bar)
- Text overlays bold white or gold, top-third of frame
- No music. No B-roll. Single continuous shot.
- The power of this ad is in the simplicity. Do not over-edit.
- Trim to exactly 20-25 seconds. Every second counts.
