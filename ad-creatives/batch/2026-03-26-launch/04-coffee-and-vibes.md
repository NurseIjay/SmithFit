## THE HOOK (0-3 seconds)

**On-screen text:** "Living off coffee and vibes?"

**IJ says:** "If you're living off coffee and vibes after a 12-hour shift..."

**Visual:** IJ in casual clothes, slightly smiling. Relaxed, not intense. This ad has a different energy than 01-03 -- it's lighter, more conversational. Like catching someone's eye in the break room.

**Sound:** IJ's voice only. The slight smile in his voice is the hook.

---

## THE PROBLEM (3-7 seconds)

**IJ says:** "...your scrubs are tight, your rings are tight, and you're blaming yourself..."

**Visual:** Same shot. IJ's expression shifts to knowing empathy -- he's describing her life back to her.

**On-screen text:** "Scrubs tight. Rings tight."

---

## THE MECHANISM + VALIDATION (7-12 seconds)

**IJ says:** "Can I say something? It's not you. It's your cortisol."

**Visual:** IJ leans slightly forward. "Can I say something?" is delivered like he's about to share a secret. "It's not you. It's your cortisol." is warm, direct, matter-of-fact.

**On-screen text:** "It's not you. It's your cortisol."

---

## THE PROOF + CTA (12-18 seconds)

**IJ says:** "And I built something for exactly this. It's $27. It takes 7 days. And 87% of nurses feel different by Day 3. Link in bio."

**Visual:** IJ returns to relaxed energy. Conversational delivery -- like recommending a restaurant, not selling.

**On-screen text (final frame, hold 3 seconds):**
```
$27 | 7 days
87% feel different by Day 3
Link in bio
```

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Setting:** Casual setting. Kitchen, living room, or outside. Somewhere that feels "off duty."

**Wardrobe:** Casual clothes. NOT scrubs. This ad is meeting her where she is -- at home, after a shift, scrolling her phone.

**Energy level:** Light, warm, slightly humorous. Like a friend leaning over and saying "hey, can I tell you something?" This is the personality ad -- it shows IJ is a real person, not a marketing machine.

**Camera:** Phone on tripod. Vertical (9:16). Slightly wider framing than the other talking-head ads to show the casual setting.

**Editing notes:**
- Auto-captions with white text on dark background bar
- Text overlays in gold or white, top-third
- No music. The warmth is in IJ's delivery.
- Keep it tight -- 15-18 seconds is the sweet spot for this ad. Cut any dead air.
- This is the shortest ad in the batch. Every word earns its place.
