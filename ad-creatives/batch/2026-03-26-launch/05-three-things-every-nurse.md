## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Setting:** No setting needed for IJ -- this is voiceover only. He records audio separately (or films himself talking and uses just the audio track).

**B-roll options (film on phone):**
- Scrubs laid out on a bed
- Coffee being poured
- Badge lanyard on a counter
- Hands tying scrub pants
- Walking through a parking lot
- Any nurse-life moment that feels authentic

**Energy level:** Educational and authoritative. Like IJ is giving a quick in-service to colleagues. Calm confidence, not hype.

**Camera:** B-roll is handheld phone footage. Vertical (9:16). Natural lighting.

**Editing notes:**
- This ad is TEXT-DRIVEN. The on-screen text is the primary content -- voiceover supports it.
- Each "point" gets a clean transition (cut or fade). 3 distinct visual beats.
- Text should be large enough to read on a phone (at least 48pt equivalent).
- Auto-captions on the voiceover as well.
- Final CTA slide should match SmithFit gold/cream brand aesthetic.
- This format earns saves and shares, which are the two signals Meta's algorithm values most.
