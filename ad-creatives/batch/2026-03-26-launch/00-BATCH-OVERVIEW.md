## Production Requirements

### IJ Filming Session (Ads 01, 02, 03, 04, 05, 07, 08)
- **Setup:** Phone on tripod, vertical (9:16), natural light, simple background
- **Wardrobe:** Scrubs (01, 03, 07, 08) and casual (02, 04, 05)
- **Audio:** Quiet room. Lapel mic recommended but phone mic acceptable.
- **Time:** 60-90 minutes for all 7 scripts. Multiple takes each.
- **Deliverable:** Raw footage to editor for captions, overlays, trimming

### Client UGC Collection (Ads 06, 09)
- Send filming instructions to 3-5 top clients
- Vertical format, phone-filmed, natural lighting, 15-30 seconds
- Written consent required before publishing
- "Results may vary" disclaimer required on all testimonial ads

### Graphic Design (Ads 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15)
- Gold/cream palette, clean typography, SmithFit logo small in corner
- Sizes needed: 1080x1080 (feed), 1080x1920 (stories), 1200x628 (Facebook)
- Tool: Canva or Figma using SmithFit brand kit

- [ ] Lead magnet quiz webhook URL configured

- [ ] $27 course purchase page live and mobile-optimized
- [ ] "Results may vary" and "not medical advice" disclaimers on landing page
