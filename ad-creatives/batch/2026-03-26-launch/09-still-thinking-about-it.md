## PRODUCTION NOTES

**This ad targets people who have ALREADY seen SmithFit content** (watched 25%+ of a video ad, visited the purchase page, engaged with an Instagram post). They know what SmithFit is. They need the final push.

### Filming Instructions for Client

> Hey [name] -- would you be willing to film a quick 15-20 second video for us? Here's what we need:
>
> 1. Film on your phone, vertical. Prop it up somewhere.
> 2. Good lighting -- face a window or sit in a bright room.
> 3. Start by saying you almost didn't sign up (what held you back).
> 4. Then say what changed your mind and what happened.
> 5. End with what you'd tell someone who's on the fence.
> 6. Keep it under 20 seconds. Your own words -- don't memorize anything.
>
> Thank you -- your story will help another nurse make the decision.

**Wardrobe:** Whatever the client is comfortable in. Scrubs are ideal but not required.

**Energy level:** Warm, conversational, authentic. Like she's talking to a coworker at the nurses' station.

**Editing notes:**
- Auto-captions (critical)
- Text overlays for timeline markers (Day 3, Week 2)
- "Results may vary" must appear on-screen and in caption
- Keep raw. Do NOT over-edit. Imperfection = trust.
- No music. No branded graphics except final CTA frame.
- This ad should be SHORT -- 15-18 seconds is the sweet spot for retargeting.
