# Concept 08: Metabolism Quiz -- B-Roll
**Funnel Stage:** BOF (Hot)
**Format:** B-Roll with IJ Voiceover
**Duration:** 22-26 seconds
**Objective:** Emotionally driven quiz teaser -- not BuzzFeed energy, nurse-who-cares energy

---

## Shot List

### SHOT 1 -- Nurse Staring at Mirror (Hook -- Emotional)
- **What to film:** Medium shot of someone in scrubs standing in front of a bathroom mirror, looking at themselves with a flat expression. Not posing -- just looking. The "nothing's working" moment.
- **Camera angle:** Over-the-shoulder into mirror
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Bathroom light, slightly harsh but warm
- **Props:** Scrubs, bathroom mirror

### SHOT 2 -- Four Type Cards
- **What to film:** Overhead shot of four cards laid out on a clean surface. Each card has a stress type name:
  - "THE CRASH-AND-BURN"
  - "THE SLOW BURN"
  - "THE WIRED AND TIRED"
  - "THE PLATEAU"
  A hand hovers over them, as if choosing.
- **Camera angle:** Overhead bird's eye
- **Movement:** Static, hand enters and hovers
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Bright, warm, clean
- **Props:** Four printed cards with type names, clean surface (white or cream)

### SHOT 3 -- Nurse Scrolling Phone on Break
- **What to film:** Medium shot of someone in scrubs sitting down (break room feel), scrolling phone, pausing to read something. Engaged -- like she found something that speaks to her.
- **Camera angle:** Medium from across the table
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Lighting:** Fluorescent or natural break-room light
- **Props:** Scrubs, phone, table, coffee cup

### SHOT 4 -- Quiz Result Screen
- **What to film:** Close-up of a phone screen showing a quiz result: "Your Type: The Crash-and-Burn" with a short description. The viewer sees what the result looks like.
- **Camera angle:** Tight on phone screen
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Phone screen glow + ambient
- **Props:** Phone with mock quiz result loaded

### SHOT 5 -- IJ Writing Stress Types
- **What to film:** IJ writing "4 STRESS TYPES" on a whiteboard and listing them:
  1. Crash-and-Burn
  2. Slow Burn
  3. Wired & Tired
  4. Plateau
- **Camera angle:** Close-up on whiteboard
- **Movement:** Following hand
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Clean, warm
- **Props:** Whiteboard, marker

### SHOT 6 -- IJ to Camera (Close)
- **What to film:** IJ to camera, warm, genuine. Not quiz-show host -- nurse offering help. "Take the quiz" energy that feels personal.
- **Camera angle:** Chest-up, eye level
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 4 seconds
- **Props:** Scrubs or casual

## A/B Variations

### Variation A (Default -- emotional)
- Opens with nurse in mirror (the "nothing's working" visual)
- Voiceover: "If you're a nurse over 35 and nothing's working..."

### Variation B (Card-reveal opening)
- Opens with Shot 2 (four type cards) -- visual of choosing
- Voiceover: "Crash-and-Burn. Slow Burn. Wired and Tired. Plateau. Which one are you? 2 minutes. Free."
- On-screen text: Each type name appears one at a time
- Then continues with Shots 1, 3-6

## Complete Filming Guide

### Summary
- **Total shots:** 6
- **Estimated filming time:** 20-25 minutes
- **Locations:** 1 (home interior)

### Props Checklist
- [ ] Scrubs + bathroom mirror
- [ ] Four printed cards with stress type names
- [ ] Clean surface (white or cream)
- [ ] Scrubs + phone + coffee cup (break scene)
- [ ] Phone with mock quiz result screen
- [ ] Whiteboard + marker
- [ ] Clean background for IJ shot

### Editing Notes
- **Cut pacing:** Medium. Inviting, not punchy.
- **Color grade:** Warm throughout. Shot 1 slightly cooler (the "before" moment), rest warm.
- **Captions:** Full hardcoded.
- **Music:** Subtle, warm ambient. Not quiz-show cheesy. Think "someone is about to understand something about themselves."
- **Key visual:** The mirror shot (Shot 1) should feel private and real. The type cards (Shot 2) should be clearly readable on mobile.
