# Concept 08: Metabolism Quiz -- Talking Head
**Funnel Stage:** BOF (Hot)
**Format:** Talking Head (IJ to camera)
**Duration:** 20-24 seconds
**Objective:** Drive quiz lead capture with EMOTIONAL hook, not flat quiz framing

---

## Script (Word-for-Word)

### HOOK (0-4 sec)
**IJ says:** "If you're a nurse over 35 and nothing's working -- I can tell you why in 2 minutes."

**On-screen text overlay:** "I can tell you why in 2 minutes."
**IJ's energy/expression:** Direct, personal. Not quiz-show energy -- nurse-who-cares energy. Leans in. "Nothing's working" -- acknowledges the pain. "I can tell you why" -- confident, personal promise.

---

### PROBLEM VALIDATION (4-10 sec)
**IJ says:** "Here's the thing. You've been blaming yourself. Thinking your body is broken. Thinking you're the one person nothing works for. You're not. Your cortisol responds differently based on your stress type -- and that changes everything about what's going to help."

**On-screen text overlay:** "Your stress type determines what works" (at 6 sec)
**IJ's energy/expression:** Validating, warm. "Thinking your body is broken" -- empathy. "You're not" -- firm, direct. "Changes everything" -- this is the pivot to curiosity.

---

### VALUE PROP (10-16 sec)
**IJ says:** "I built a free 2-minute quiz that tells you your type and gives you a strategy that actually matches your life. I'm an RN. I designed it for nurses who work shifts, not 9-to-5."

**On-screen text overlay:** "Free quiz | 2 minutes | Built by an RN"
**IJ's energy/expression:** Generous energy. This is free value. Not a pitch -- an offer from someone who gets it. Pride in the credential.

---

### CTA (16-22 sec)
**IJ says:** "Take the free quiz -- link in bio. Takes 2 minutes. No purchase required. You'll finally know why nothing's been working."

**On-screen text overlay:** "FREE QUIZ -- LINK IN BIO | 2 minutes | No purchase required"
**IJ's energy/expression:** Simple, direct. "You'll finally know why" -- the emotional payoff. She's been wondering. This gives her the answer. Warm, inviting close.

## Production Notes

### Setting
- Clean, bright background. Home or office. Inviting, not clinical.

### Wardrobe
- Scrubs (reinforces RN credential for quiz credibility) or clean casual.

### Camera
- iPhone, tripod, vertical 9:16, chest-up, eye level.

### Lighting
- Bright, warm natural light. Friendly, approachable energy.

### Audio
- Lapel mic. Quiet room.

### Editing
- **Captions:** Hardcoded, full. Bold white.
- **Text overlays:** Key stats and the "free" message. "FREE" should be impossible to miss.
- **Cuts:** Minimal. 0-1 jump cuts.
- **Music:** None or extremely subtle upbeat ambient.
- **End frame:** CTA overlay holds 4 seconds (longer than other ads -- give them time to act on quiz CTA).
