# Concept 10: Feel Like Yourself Again -- Talking Head
**Funnel Stage:** BOF (Hot)
**Format:** Talking Head (IJ to camera)
**Duration:** 26-32 seconds
**Objective:** Emotional identity close with fear-before-hope, "put together without trying," belonging, urgency, "not the exception"

---

## Script (Word-for-Word)

**IJ says:** "The scariest thought isn't 'I need to lose weight.' It's 'what if nothing ever works for me?'"

**On-screen text overlay:** "What if nothing ever works for me?"
**IJ's energy/expression:** Quiet, serious. This is the deepest fear. Deliver it slowly. Eye contact. Pause after the question -- let the viewer feel it in her chest. This is not a hook. This is a mirror.

## Production Notes

### Setting
- Quiet room. Simple, clean. Warm light. This is the most intimate ad in the batch -- the setting should feel private and calm.

### Wardrobe
- Clean casual. Solid color. This isn't about the RN credential -- it's about human connection. No scrubs for this one.

### Camera
- iPhone, tripod, vertical 9:16. Chest-up. Eye level. Slightly closer than other ads -- 1.5-2 feet. Intimate framing.

### Lighting
- Warm, soft. Golden hour or warm lamp. No harsh lighting. Flattering.

### Audio
- Lapel mic. Quiet room. The softness of the delivery demands clean audio.

### Editing
- **Captions:** Hardcoded. Bold white. But slightly smaller than other ads -- the visuals should feel calm, not aggressive.
- **Text overlays:** Minimal. One phrase at a time. Gentle appearance (fade in, not snap in).
- **Cuts:** Zero. Single take. The unbroken eye contact IS the creative.
- **Color grade:** Warm golden tone throughout. Soft. Slightly lower contrast than other ads.
- **Music:** Soft, warm piano or acoustic guitar. Very subtle -- barely audible. Enhances the emotional tone.
- **Pacing:** Slower than all other ads. Let pauses breathe. This is not a rapid-fire piece -- it's a moment.
