# Concept 14: The Comparison Trap -- Talking Head
**Funnel Stage:** TOF (Cold)
**Format:** Talking Head (IJ to camera)
**Duration:** 26-32 seconds
**Objective:** Name the comparison shame she feels around other nurses, relieve it with biology, offer the reset as the right tool for her specific fight

---

## Script (Word-for-Word)

### HOOK (0-5 sec)
**IJ says:** "That nurse you work with -- the one who meal preps every Sunday, hits the gym before shift, looks good in her scrubs. You look at her and wonder what's wrong with you."

**On-screen text overlay:** "What's wrong with me?"
**IJ's energy/expression:** Knowing. Empathetic. Not sad -- like he's describing a thought she has every week. "You look at her and wonder what's wrong with you" should be delivered as recognition, not accusation. He's naming a silent pain. Pause after.

---

### VALIDATION (5-10 sec)
**IJ says:** "Nothing. Nothing is wrong with you. Her schedule, her age, her stress load -- they're different. She might be 28, working straight days, no kids. That's not a fair comparison."

**On-screen text overlay:** "Nothing is wrong with you."
**IJ's energy/expression:** Immediate relief. "Nothing" is delivered fast, like he's cutting off the bad thought before it takes root. Then he slows down for the explanation. "That's not a fair comparison" -- said plainly, like it's obvious. Because to him, it is.

---

### MECHANISM (10-18 sec)
**IJ says:** "Here's what nobody tells you. After 35, when you've been doing 12-hour shifts for years, your cortisol -- your stress hormones -- they're running at a completely different level than hers. Your body is fighting a different fight. And the tools that work for her aren't going to work for you. Your body needs something built for where you actually are."

**On-screen text overlay:** "Your body is fighting a different fight." (appears at 14 sec)
**IJ's energy/expression:** Education mode. Clear, patient. Not lecturing -- explaining something he wishes someone had explained to him. "Your body is fighting a different fight" gets a slight emphasis. He believes this.

---

### REFRAME (18-24 sec)
**IJ says:** "You're not behind. You're not broken. Stop comparing your chapter 15 to someone else's chapter 3. You need the right tools for your fight. And that's what I built."

**On-screen text overlay:** "Stop comparing your chapter 15 to someone else's chapter 3."
**IJ's energy/expression:** Warm conviction. "Stop comparing" is not a command -- it's permission. "And that's what I built" is said with quiet pride. He's been exactly where she is.

---

### CTA (24-30 sec)
**IJ says:** "The Shift-Proof Metabolism Reset. Built by a nurse for nurses over 35 dealing with real stress. $27. 60-day guarantee. Link in bio."

**On-screen text overlay:** "$27 | 60-day guarantee | Link in bio"
**IJ's energy/expression:** Direct, simple. The emotional peak was the reframe. The CTA is calm delivery. Brief nod.

## Production Notes

### Setting
- **Location:** Home environment. Kitchen counter or living room. Casual.
- **Background:** Warm, simple. The conversation is the production value.
- **Vibe:** Friend-to-friend. He noticed she does this and is gently calling it out.

### Wardrobe
- **Option 1 (preferred):** Scrubs. Reinforces "I'm one of you, I've seen this comparison on the floor."
- **Option 2:** Clean casual tee.

### Camera
- **Device:** iPhone on tripod
- **Framing:** Chest-up, centered, vertical (9:16 / 1080x1920)
- **Angle:** Eye level. Standard distance -- 2-2.5 feet.

### Lighting
- **Primary:** Natural window light. Warm, clean.
- **Avoid:** Anything too dim or moody. This is a relief ad, not a somber one.

### Audio
- **Mic:** Lapel mic. Clean audio.
- **Environment:** Quiet room.

### Editing
- **Captions:** Hardcoded, bold white with dark shadow. Full transcript.
- **Text overlays:** "Nothing is wrong with you" should fade in gently. "Stop comparing your chapter 15..." should snap in -- it's a punchline.
- **Cuts:** One or two jump cuts to tighten. The hook section is dense and needs clean delivery.
- **Color grade:** Warm, natural. Standard SmithFit warmth.
- **Music:** None, or barely-there ambient warmth. The voice carries this one.
- **Pacing:** Medium. The hook is fast (describing the scenario). The validation slows down (relief). Mechanism is steady (education). Reframe punches. CTA is calm.
- **End frame:** CTA overlay holds 3 seconds.
