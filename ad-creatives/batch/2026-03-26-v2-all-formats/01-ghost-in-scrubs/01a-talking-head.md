# Concept 01: Ghost in Scrubs -- Talking Head
**Funnel Stage:** TOF (Cold)
**Format:** Talking Head (IJ to camera)
**Duration:** 30-35 seconds
**Objective:** Introduce IJ's origin story, build trust, name the trajectory fear, drive engagement

---

## Script (Word-for-Word)

### HOOK (0-3 sec)
**IJ says:** "Two years ago, I was an RN working 12-hour shifts and I felt like a ghost in my own body."

**On-screen text overlay:** "I felt like a ghost in my own body"
**IJ's energy/expression:** Reflective, vulnerable, slight pause after "ghost." Eye contact locked on camera. Not smiling -- genuine.

---

### PROBLEM (3-12 sec)
**IJ says:** "I'd tried everything. Keto. Intermittent fasting. More cardio. Nothing stuck. And every month it was getting worse. Tighter scrubs. Less energy. I could see where this was heading. And I blamed myself every single time."

**On-screen text overlay:** "Tried everything. Nothing stuck." (at 3 sec) then "Every month it was getting worse." (at 7 sec)
**IJ's energy/expression:** Builds frustration gradually. Slight head shake on "nothing stuck." Slows down on "I could see where this was heading" -- let the trajectory fear land. Controlled intensity, not dramatic.

---

**On-screen text overlay:** "It wasn't the food. It was cortisol." (appears at 14 sec)
**IJ's energy/expression:** Shift to clarity. Leans in slightly on "solving the wrong problem." Calm authority on "calm my nervous system down first."

---

**IJ's energy/expression:** Confident, warm. Pride without arrogance. "Ghost to grounded" -- slight nod, let it land. This is the identity shift line.

## Production Notes

### Setting
- **Location:** Home office or clean, simple room. NOT a gym. NOT overly styled.
- **Background:** Neutral wall, slight depth (bookshelf or plant slightly blurred). Nothing distracting.
- **Vibe:** Looks like IJ grabbed his phone and is talking to a friend. Authentic, not produced.

### Wardrobe
- **Option 1 (preferred):** Scrubs (navy or dark gray). Badge lanyard visible on neck or clipped to pocket.
- **Option 2:** Clean casual -- solid color t-shirt (black, gray, or navy). No logos.

### Camera
- **Device:** iPhone on tripod (or propped at eye level)
- **Framing:** Chest-up, centered, vertical (9:16 / 1080x1920)
- **Angle:** Straight-on, eye level. No looking up or down at camera.
- **Distance:** Arm's length to 3 feet. Close enough to feel personal.

### Lighting
- **Primary:** Natural window light from the front or 45 degrees to one side
- **Avoid:** Overhead fluorescent (looks institutional), backlighting (face in shadow)
- **Time of day:** Morning or afternoon daylight preferred

### Audio
- **Mic:** Lapel mic clipped to scrub collar or shirt. If no lapel mic, film in a quiet room with phone mic.
- **Environment:** No background TV, no AC hum, no traffic. Close windows.

### Editing
- **Captions:** Hardcoded, bold white text with dark shadow/background bar. Full sentence captions synced to speech.
- **Text overlays:** Appear as noted above, 2-3 seconds each, positioned top-center or mid-left
- **Cuts:** Minimal. This is a straight-to-camera piece. One or two jump cuts OK to tighten pacing but not styled/flashy.
- **Color grade:** Warm, natural. Slight warmth boost. No heavy filters.
- **Music:** None, or extremely subtle ambient track (barely audible). Voice is the focus.
- **End frame:** Hold the CTA overlay for 3 full seconds after IJ stops speaking.
