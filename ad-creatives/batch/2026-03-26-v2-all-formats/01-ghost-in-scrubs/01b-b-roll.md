# Concept 01: Ghost in Scrubs -- B-Roll
**Funnel Stage:** TOF (Cold)
**Format:** B-Roll with IJ Voiceover
**Duration:** 32-36 seconds
**Objective:** Cinematic nurse-life visuals with IJ's origin story voiceover, trajectory fear, identity shift

---

## Shot List

### SHOT 1 -- Hospital Parking Lot (Hook)
- **What to film:** IJ walking through a dimly lit parking lot toward a car, shot from behind. Scrubs visible. Badge lanyard swinging. End-of-shift posture -- shoulders slightly down, tired gait.
- **Camera angle:** Medium-wide from behind, slow follow. Camera at waist height looking slightly up.
- **Movement:** Slow tracking follow shot
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Low ambient -- streetlights or early dawn. Slightly cool/blue tone.
- **Props:** Scrubs (dark), badge lanyard, car keys in hand, water bottle or lunch bag

### SHOT 2 -- Car Mirror Reflection
- **What to film:** Close-up of IJ's face reflected in the rearview mirror of a parked car. Tired eyes. He pauses and looks at himself, then looks away.
- **Camera angle:** Tight close-up of the rearview mirror, shot from backseat perspective
- **Movement:** Static, locked off
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Interior car, ambient light from parking lot or dashboard glow
- **Props:** Car interior, rearview mirror

### SHOT 3 -- Kitchen Counter at Night
- **What to film:** Overhead shot of a kitchen counter at night: a takeout container half-eaten, a phone showing 2:37 AM, a coffee mug.
- **Camera angle:** Directly overhead (bird's eye), looking straight down at counter
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 2.5 seconds
- **Lighting:** Single overhead kitchen light. Everything else dark. Harsh, isolating feel.
- **Props:** Takeout container (Chinese/pizza box), phone with time visible, coffee mug, badge lanyard tossed on counter

### SHOT 3B -- Charting at 2 AM (NEW)
- **What to film:** Close-up of hands typing on a keyboard or writing in a chart at a desk. Dim light from a monitor. An empty snack wrapper and cold coffee sit nearby. The clock or phone in frame reads 2:15 AM. This is the "you had to be there" night-shift shot.
- **Camera angle:** Close-up overhead on the desk, hands and keyboard/chart visible
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 2.5 seconds
- **Lighting:** Single monitor glow or dim desk lamp. Blue-tinted. Everything else dark.
- **Props:** Keyboard or chart, pen, snack wrapper, coffee cup (half-empty, cold), phone or clock showing 2:15 AM

### SHOT 4 -- Scrubs in Mirror
- **What to film:** Medium shot of IJ standing in front of a bathroom mirror, pulling at the waist of his scrubs. Subtle gesture of clothes not fitting right. Face partially visible in mirror.
- **Camera angle:** Over-the-shoulder into the mirror, slightly off-center
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 2.5 seconds
- **Lighting:** Bathroom vanity lights -- warm, slightly harsh
- **Props:** Scrubs, bathroom mirror

### SHOT 5 -- Hands on Desk with Research
- **What to film:** Close-up of hands turning pages of a medical journal or printed research article. A highlighter marks a passage about cortisol. A coffee mug sits nearby.
- **Camera angle:** Overhead close-up on the hands and papers
- **Movement:** Slight push-in (zoom) toward the highlighted text
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Warm desk lamp, shallow depth of field
- **Props:** Printed research articles/medical journal, yellow highlighter, coffee mug, reading glasses

### SHOT 6 -- Morning Routine Reset
- **What to film:** IJ mixing the metabolic cocktail (glass of water with lemon, pinch of salt). Calm, intentional movement. Sunlight coming through kitchen window.
- **Camera angle:** Medium close-up on hands and glass, 45-degree angle
- **Movement:** Static with slight handheld drift (organic feel)
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Warm morning sunlight through window -- golden hour feel. Sharp contrast to the night kitchen shot (Shot 3).
- **Props:** Clear glass, lemon wedge, small salt container, clean kitchen counter, morning light

### SHOT 7 -- IJ to Camera (Mechanism Reveal)
- **What to film:** IJ speaking directly to camera, mid-sentence, calm and confident. Scrubs or casual. This is the "face reveal" moment where the voiceover connects to a real person.
- **Camera angle:** Chest-up, straight-on, eye level
- **Movement:** Static (tripod)
- **Duration:** 4 seconds
- **Lighting:** Natural window light, warm
- **Props:** None -- clean background

### SHOT 8 -- Phone Screen with Community
- **What to film:** Close-up of a phone screen showing a community feed or group chat with messages like "Day 3 and I feel amazing" and "My scrubs fit!" Thumb scrolling slowly through messages.
- **Camera angle:** Over-the-shoulder close-up on the phone screen
- **Movement:** Static, phone scrolls
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Natural ambient
- **Props:** Phone with community screenshots loaded (can be mocked up)

### SHOT 9 -- Walking with Confidence (Closing)
- **What to film:** IJ walking toward camera in a well-lit hallway or outdoor path. Good posture, slight smile, scrubs or casual. Confident energy -- the "after" without being a before/after.
- **Camera angle:** Medium-wide, straight-on, low angle (slightly below eye level -- empowering)
- **Movement:** IJ walks toward stationary camera
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Bright, warm natural light. Overexposed slightly for hopeful/aspirational tone.
- **Props:** Scrubs or clean casual outfit

## Complete Filming Guide

### Summary
- **Total shots:** 10
- **Estimated filming time:** 50-65 minutes (including setup and multiple takes)
- **Locations needed:** 3 (parking lot/outdoor, home interior, car)
- **Wardrobe:** Scrubs (dark navy/gray) + badge lanyard

### Props Checklist
- [ ] Scrubs (dark navy or gray)
- [ ] Badge lanyard with ID badge
- [ ] Car keys
- [ ] Water bottle or lunch bag
- [ ] Takeout container (Chinese/pizza)
- [ ] Phone showing 2:37 AM on lock screen
- [ ] Coffee mug (2 scenes -- night kitchen and research desk)
- [ ] Badge lanyard (tossed on counter for Shot 3)
- [ ] Keyboard or chart, pen, snack wrapper (Shot 3B -- charting at 2 AM)
- [ ] Phone or clock showing 2:15 AM (Shot 3B)
- [ ] Medical journal or printed research articles
- [ ] Yellow highlighter
- [ ] Reading glasses (optional)
- [ ] Clear glass, lemon wedge, small salt container
- [ ] Phone with community chat screenshots loaded
- [ ] Clean casual outfit (optional alternate wardrobe for Shot 7/9)

### Location Checklist
- [ ] Parking lot (hospital or any large lot with streetlights) -- film at dusk or dawn
- [ ] Car interior (any car, dashboard visible)
- [ ] Kitchen -- cleared counter, overhead light accessible
- [ ] Desk area with dim light, keyboard or chart (for charting at 2 AM shot)
- [ ] Bathroom mirror
- [ ] Desk area with desk lamp (research shot)
- [ ] Kitchen window (morning light for Shot 6)
- [ ] Clean background wall/room (for Shot 7)
- [ ] Well-lit hallway or outdoor walking path (for Shot 9)

### Editing Notes
- **Cut pacing:** Shots 1-4 slightly slower (reflective, somber). Shots 5-9 pick up pace (discovery, hope, confidence).
- **Color grade:** Shots 1-4: cooler, slightly desaturated (the "before" feeling). Shots 5-9: warmer, brighter (the "after" feeling). Transition happens at Shot 5.
- **Captions:** Full hardcoded captions on all voiceover. Bold white with dark background bar. Positioned lower third.
- **Music:** Subtle piano or ambient track. Starts quiet/melancholy (Shots 1-4), shifts to warmer/hopeful tone (Shots 5-9). Never louder than voiceover.
- **Sound design:** Optional -- subtle parking lot ambience on Shot 1, keyboard clicks on Shot 3B, coffee pour sound on Shot 6.
