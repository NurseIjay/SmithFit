# Concept 07: Objection Buster -- B-Roll
**Funnel Stage:** MOF (Warm)
**Format:** B-Roll with IJ Voiceover (rapid-fire 8-objection handling)
**Duration:** 34-38 seconds
**Objective:** Visual objection-answer sequence with nurse-life B-roll pairing all 8 objections

---

## Shot List

### SHOT 1 -- Text Card: Hook (Designed, not filmed)
- **Style:** Gold text on dark background: "LET ME HANDLE YOUR OBJECTIONS. ALL 8." with fade-in.
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Style:** Sans-serif bold, 48px, #C5A55A on #2D2D2D

### SHOT 2 -- Night Parking Lot (Objection 1: Night Shifts)
- **What to film:** Quick shot of a hospital parking lot at night. Streetlights. A figure in scrubs walking.
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Props:** Scrubs, parking lot

### SHOT 3 -- Whiteboard Answer 1
- **What to film:** IJ's hand writing "SHIFT-BASED" and circling it.
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Props:** Whiteboard, marker

### SHOT 4 -- Messy Kitchen Counter (Objection 2: Meal Prep)
- **What to film:** Overhead of cluttered kitchen: half-packed lunch, scattered ingredients, phone showing the time.
- **Duration:** 2 seconds

### SHOT 5 -- Three Template Cards
- **What to film:** "WORK DAY" / "OFF DAY" / "CHAOS DAY" cards. Hand taps Chaos Day.
- **Duration:** 2 seconds

### SHOT 6 -- Exhausted Couch Sit (Objection 3: Tried Everything)
- **What to film:** Someone in scrubs on couch, head in hands. Post-shift defeated posture.
- **Duration:** 1.5 seconds

### SHOT 7 -- IJ to Camera (Answer 3)
- **What to film:** IJ delivering the line with conviction. Firm but empathetic.
- **Duration:** 2 seconds

### SHOT 8 -- Reading Glasses (Objection 4: Menopause)
- **What to film:** Close-up of a hand holding reading glasses. Dignified, not defeated.
- **Duration:** 1.5 seconds

### SHOT 9 -- Morning Cocktail (Answer 4)
- **What to film:** Metabolic cocktail being prepared (water + lemon + salt). Calm, intentional.
- **Duration:** 2 seconds

### SHOT 10 -- Calorie App Close + Delete (Objection 5: Macros)
- **What to film:** Close-up of phone showing a calorie tracking app. Finger swipes to close/delete it.
- **Duration:** 1.5 seconds

### SHOT 11 -- 15-Min Timer (Objection 6: Too Exhausted)
- **What to film:** Phone timer being set to 15:00. Simple, low-key. "This is all it takes."
- **Duration:** 1.5 seconds

### SHOT 12 -- Workbook Page (Objection 7: Meal Plan)
- **What to film:** Overhead of a workbook or printed page showing "Shift-Proof System" with structured templates visible. Not a meal plan -- a system.
- **Duration:** 1.5 seconds

### SHOT 13 -- Guarantee Card (Objection 8: Fail Again)
- **What to film:** A printed card or sticky note reading "60-DAY MONEY-BACK GUARANTEE" being placed on a clean surface.
- **Duration:** 2 seconds

### SHOT 14 -- IJ Confident Close
- **What to film:** IJ to camera, confident nod, slight smile. "Eight objections. Zero standing."
- **Duration:** 3 seconds

## A/B Variations

### Variation A (Default)
- Opens with text card "LET ME HANDLE YOUR OBJECTIONS"
- 8 objections in order

### Variation B (Start with the strongest)
- Opens with Shot 6 (exhausted couch) + "But I've tried everything"
- Voiceover: "'I've tried everything.' I hear this every day. And here's what I say..."
- Then handles the other 7 objections. Ends the same.

## Complete Filming Guide

### Summary
- **Total shots:** 14 (Shot 1 is designed, not filmed)
- **Estimated filming time:** 35-45 minutes
- **Locations:** 2 (home interior, parking lot)

### Props Checklist
- [ ] Scrubs
- [ ] Parking lot access (night)
- [ ] Whiteboard + marker
- [ ] Cluttered kitchen (messy counter, lunch container, food)
- [ ] Three printed cards: WORK DAY / OFF DAY / CHAOS DAY
- [ ] Couch
- [ ] Reading glasses + book or computer
- [ ] Glass, water, lemon, salt
- [ ] Phone with calorie tracking app
- [ ] Phone timer set to 15:00
- [ ] Workbook or printed "Shift-Proof System" page
- [ ] Printed "60-DAY GUARANTEE" card
- [ ] Clean background for IJ shots

### Editing Notes
- **Cut pacing:** FAST. 1.5-2 seconds per shot. Rapid-fire Q&A energy.
- **Objection/answer structure:** Each objection in white italic text, then cuts to answer in gold bold text. Pattern repeats 8 times.
- **Color grade:** Consistent, bright. No mood shifts.
- **Music:** Rhythmic, percussive beat at low volume. Cuts land on the beat.
- **Captions:** Hardcoded. Full transcript. Bold.
