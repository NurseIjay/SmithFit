## Production Notes

### Setting
- Clean background. Home office or neutral wall. Same setup works for all talking head ads.

### Wardrobe
- Scrubs preferred. The RN authority matters here -- every objection answer carries more weight in scrubs.

### Camera
- iPhone on tripod. Vertical 9:16. Chest-up. Eye level.

### Lighting
- Bright, even natural light. No mood lighting -- this is punchy and direct.

### Audio
- Lapel mic. Quiet room.

### Editing
- **THIS AD REQUIRES JUMP CUTS.** Each objection is a quick-cut -- film each objection/answer as a separate take. The cuts between objections should be sharp and snappy.
- **Captions:** Hardcoded. Bold white. The objection text appears first (italic, in quotes) then the answer appears in gold.
- **Text overlays:** Each objection gets its own text treatment. The objection is in white italic, the answer is in gold bold.
- **Color grade:** Bright, consistent. No shifts.
- **Music:** Fast-paced but subtle. A rhythmic beat or percussive track at 10-15% volume helps sell the rapid-fire pace. Cuts on the beat.
- **Pacing:** Tight. No dead air between objections. Each answer should start within 0.5 seconds of the objection ending.
