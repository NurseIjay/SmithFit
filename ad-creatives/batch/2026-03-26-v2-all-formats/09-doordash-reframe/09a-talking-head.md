# Concept 09: DoorDash Reframe -- Talking Head
**Funnel Stage:** BOF (Hot)
**Format:** Talking Head (IJ to camera)
**Duration:** 20-24 seconds
**Objective:** Price-anchor $27, family objection handling, urgency, quiet transformation, "not the exception"

---

## Script (Word-for-Word)

### HOOK (0-3 sec)
**IJ says:** "$27. That's less than one DoorDash order after a night shift."

**On-screen text overlay:** "$27. Less than DoorDash."
**IJ's energy/expression:** Direct, matter-of-fact. Slight head tilt. Like stating an obvious comparison she hasn't considered.

---

**IJ says:** "For $27 you get a 7-Day Shift-Proof Metabolism Reset built by an RN. The metabolic cocktail protocol. Shift-proof nutrition templates. Box breathing for cortisol. And a 60-day money-back guarantee."

**On-screen text overlay (stacking):**

- "Box breathing" (at 7 sec)
- "60-day guarantee" (at 8 sec)
**IJ's energy/expression:** Listing off confidently. Each item gets a subtle finger count. Building the value stack. Not rushed -- each item lands.

---

### PROOF + URGENCY (10-16 sec)
**IJ says:** "400 nurses have done it. 87% feel different by Day 3. You're not the exception. And if someone at home thinks it's another waste of money -- there's a 60-day guarantee. If it doesn't work, you get your money back. No crazy protocol. No 5 AM workouts. A quiet reset."

**IJ's energy/expression:** "You're not the exception" -- direct, firm. The family objection handling -- "someone at home" -- should feel like IJ knows her life. Not salesy. Knowing. "Quiet reset" -- softer, aspirational.

---

### CTA (16-22 sec)
**IJ says:** "Your body doesn't have a pause button. Link in bio. It's $27. Skip the DoorDash."

**On-screen text overlay:** "LINK IN BIO | $27 | Your body doesn't have a pause button."
**IJ's energy/expression:** The urgency line -- "your body doesn't have a pause button" -- not pressure, just truth. Then playful close. "Skip the DoorDash" with a slight knowing smile. Conspiratorial. Like a friend giving a tip.

## Production Notes

### Setting
- Kitchen counter with a phone visible (implies ordering food). Or simple clean background.

### Wardrobe
- Casual (this is a relatable, off-duty vibe). Solid tee.

### Camera
- iPhone, tripod, vertical 9:16, chest-up, eye level.

### Lighting
- Warm, natural. Kitchen or living room.

### Audio
- Lapel mic. Quiet room.

### Editing
- **Captions:** Hardcoded. Bold.
- **Value stack text:** Each item appears and STAYS on screen, stacking vertically. By the end of the section, all 5 items are visible simultaneously. Then they clear for the proof section.
- **Cuts:** 0-1. Keep it raw and direct.
- **Music:** None. The directness is the appeal.
- **Pacing:** Short and punchy. This is a conversion piece -- no fat.
