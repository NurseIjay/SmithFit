## Photo/Image Direction
- **Text-only design.** The crossed-out bad advice IS the visual. The strikethrough lines create instant pattern recognition -- she's seen this advice. The gold strikes through it. The contrast between the dim crossed-out lines and the bright truth section below creates the emotional shift.
- **No photos.** The starkness is the point.
