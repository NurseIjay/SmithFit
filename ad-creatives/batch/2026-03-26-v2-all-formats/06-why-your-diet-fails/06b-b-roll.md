# Concept 06: Why Your Diet Fails -- B-Roll
**Funnel Stage:** MOF (Warm)
**Format:** B-Roll with IJ Voiceover (Revelation, not lecture)
**Duration:** 36-40 seconds
**Objective:** Visual education with anger at bad advice, counterintuitive cortisol hook, personal tone

---

## Shot List

### SHOT 1 -- Generic Diet Ad Scroll (Hook)
- **What to film:** Close-up of a phone screen scrolling through generic fitness content: "Eat clean!" "5 AM workout routine!" "Meal prep Sunday!" "Calorie deficit!" Thumb scrolls past each one.
- **Camera angle:** Over-the-shoulder tight on phone
- **Movement:** Static, thumb scrolls
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Phone glow, dim room (post-shift scrolling feel)
- **Props:** Phone with Instagram feed loaded (mock generic diet/fitness screenshots)

### SHOT 2 -- Clock Timelapse
- **What to film:** A clock face (analog or digital) with the hour hand moving from 7 PM to 7 AM (or similar 12-hour span). Can be a real timelapse or sped-up video.
- **Camera angle:** Close-up, centered on clock face
- **Movement:** Static, clock moves
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Neutral, clock clearly visible
- **Props:** Wall clock or large desk clock

### SHOT 3 -- Nurse Station Activity
- **What to film:** A desk or counter with nursing items: stethoscope, pen, clipboard, medication cups. Hands moving papers. Constant activity, no rest.
- **Camera angle:** Overhead, looking down at the workspace
- **Movement:** Static, hands move through frame
- **Duration:** 2.5 seconds
- **Lighting:** Fluorescent (institutional)
- **Props:** Stethoscope, pen, clipboard or papers, medication cups, badge

### SHOT 4 -- Small Sad Meal (The Counterintuitive Setup)
- **What to film:** Overhead shot of a tiny, restrictive meal -- a small salad, a few crackers, a diet soda. Badge lanyard beside it. This is "eating less" visualized. Then IJ's hand enters frame and pushes the plate away.
- **Camera angle:** Overhead bird's eye
- **Movement:** Static, then hand enters
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Harsh fluorescent (break room feel)
- **Props:** Small plate of restrictive food, badge lanyard, IJ's hand

### SHOT 5 -- Cortisol Diagram (The Mechanism Visual)
- **What to film:** IJ's hand drawing on a whiteboard:
  - A horizontal timeline labeled "12-HR SHIFT"
  - A line above it labeled "CORTISOL" that goes up and stays elevated
  - Then a second arrow from "EATING LESS" pointing UP at the cortisol line -- making it WORSE
  - Below: arrows pointing to "FAT STORAGE" and "CRAVINGS" and "SLOW METABOLISM"
- **Camera angle:** Close-up on the drawing surface
- **Movement:** Camera slowly pushes in as diagram completes
- **Duration:** 5 seconds (the key educational shot)
- **Lighting:** Warm desk or overhead light
- **Props:** Whiteboard + colored markers

### SHOT 6 -- Failed Diet Montage
- **What to film:** Quick-cut montage (3 sub-shots, 1 second each):
  - Sub-shot A: A keto-style meal with a small "X" card
  - Sub-shot B: A phone timer showing "16:8 FAST" with an "X" card
  - Sub-shot C: Running shoes on a treadmill with an "X" card
- **Camera angle:** Each is a close-up
- **Movement:** Quick cuts, static shots
- **Duration:** 3 seconds total
- **Lighting:** Bright kitchen/gym light
- **Props:** Keto meal, phone with IF app, running shoes, three "X" cards

### SHOT 7 -- The "Calm First" Moment
- **What to film:** Close-up of IJ's hand writing "CALM FIRST" in large letters on the whiteboard. He underlines it twice.
- **Camera angle:** Close-up on the whiteboard
- **Movement:** Following the hand
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Clean, bright
- **Props:** Whiteboard, marker

### SHOT 8 -- Calm Morning Routine
- **What to film:** Sequence of calm actions: glass of water being poured, a deep breath (chest rises and falls), a journal being opened. The visual contrast to the frenetic earlier shots.
- **Camera angle:** Mixed close-ups
- **Movement:** Gentle, slow
- **Duration:** 3 seconds total
- **Lighting:** Warm morning light. Golden. Peaceful.
- **Props:** Glass of water, journal, pen, kitchen counter with morning light

### SHOT 9 -- IJ to Camera (Authority Close)
- **What to film:** IJ facing camera. Scrubs. Calm confidence. A slight nod. This isn't a lecture -- it's a friend who figured something out.
- **Camera angle:** Chest-up, eye level
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 4 seconds
- **Lighting:** Warm, natural
- **Props:** Scrubs, clean background

## Complete Filming Guide

### Summary
- **Total shots:** 9 (plus 3 sub-shots in Shot 6)
- **Estimated filming time:** 40-50 minutes
- **Locations:** 2 (home interior with whiteboard, kitchen)

### Props Checklist
- [ ] Phone with Instagram loaded (generic diet/fitness content)
- [ ] Wall clock or large desk clock
- [ ] Stethoscope, pen, clipboard, medication cups, badge
- [ ] Small restrictive meal plate (salad, crackers, diet soda)
- [ ] Whiteboard + markers (black, red/gold, green/gold)
- [ ] Keto meal plate (bacon, eggs, avocado)
- [ ] Phone with IF app screen
- [ ] Running shoes + treadmill or floor
- [ ] Three small "X" cards in red
- [ ] Glass of water
- [ ] Journal + pen
- [ ] Scrubs for IJ (Shot 9)

### Editing Notes
- **Cut pacing:** Shots 1-6 build the case -- medium to fast pacing. Shot 6 (diet montage) is quick cuts (1 sec each) for punch. Shots 7-8 are slower, deliberate (the solution). Shot 9 holds.
- **Color grade:** Shots 1-3: cooler, institutional. Shot 4: stark (the push-away moment). Shots 5-6: neutral. Shots 7-9: warmer, brighter.
- **The whiteboard:** Key visual element. The "eating less makes cortisol worse" diagram is the centerpiece. Ensure legibility on mobile.
- **Captions:** Full hardcoded. Critical -- info-dense.
- **Music:** None for Shots 1-6 (the silence adds weight to the revelations). Subtle warm ambient for Shots 7-9. The shift from silence to music IS the transition.
