# Concept 16: Nobody Needs to Know -- Talking Head
**Funnel Stage:** MOF (Warm)
**Format:** Talking Head (IJ to camera)
**Duration:** 24-30 seconds
**Objective:** Speak to the stealth transformation desire -- she doesn't want to broadcast a new diet, she wants quiet results that people just notice

---

## Script (Word-for-Word)

### HOOK (0-4 sec)
**IJ says:** "You don't have to announce a new diet. You don't have to meal prep on Instagram. Nobody needs to know."

**On-screen text overlay:** "Nobody needs to know."
**IJ's energy/expression:** Calm, almost conspiratorial. Like he's letting her in on a secret. Slight lean in. A quiet confidence. Not whispering -- but private-feeling. The energy of "I'm about to tell you something the other ads won't."

---

### THE APPEAL (4-10 sec)
**IJ says:** "No announcement. No before photo. No telling everyone at work you're 'starting something.' You just... start. Quietly. And within a couple weeks, something shifts. Your scrubs fit different. You have energy after shift. You're not reaching for coffee to survive."

**On-screen text overlay:** "Your scrubs fit different."
**IJ's energy/expression:** Painting a picture. Each detail is a snapshot of the quiet transformation she fantasizes about. "You just... start" has a pause in the middle -- the ellipsis is spoken. The description should feel like daydreaming out loud.

---

### THE MOMENT (10-16 sec)
**IJ says:** "And then someone at work says it. 'You look different. What are you doing?' And you can tell them or not. That's up to you."

**On-screen text overlay:** "'What are you doing?'"
**IJ's energy/expression:** Slight smile. This is the moment she wants. The payoff. "You can tell them or not" -- said with a knowing look. He's painting the power of a private win. "That's up to you" is delivered with warmth. She's in control.

---

### MECHANISM (16-22 sec)
**IJ says:** "The Shift-Proof Reset works in the background while you live your life. No crazy protocol. No 5 AM workouts. It calms your nervous system down and lets your metabolism do what it's supposed to do. That's it."

**On-screen text overlay:** "Works in the background."
**IJ's energy/expression:** Simple, matter-of-fact. "No crazy protocol" is tossed off casually. "That's it" is delivered with a slight shrug -- because it really is that simple. The understatement IS the sell.

---

### CTA (22-28 sec)
**IJ says:** "Seven days. $27. Built by a nurse. 60-day guarantee. You don't have to make a whole thing out of it. Just start. Link in bio."

**On-screen text overlay:** "$27 | 60-day guarantee | Link in bio"
**IJ's energy/expression:** Light, easy. "You don't have to make a whole thing out of it" should feel like relief. "Just start" is the simplest CTA in the batch. Brief smile.

## Production Notes

### Setting
- **Location:** Kitchen or living room. Warm, home. Casual.
- **Background:** Clean, simple. Nothing fitness-related. No gym equipment, no meal prep visible. The anti-fitness-ad setting.
- **Vibe:** A quiet conversation. Like telling a friend about something good that nobody else knows about yet.

### Wardrobe
- **Option 1 (preferred):** Clean casual. Solid tee. This isn't about credentials or scrubs. It's about a private moment.
- **Option 2:** Scrub pants with a casual top. The off-duty look.

### Camera
- **Device:** iPhone on tripod
- **Framing:** Chest-up, centered, vertical (9:16 / 1080x1920)
- **Angle:** Eye level. Standard distance -- 2-2.5 feet.

### Lighting
- **Primary:** Warm, natural. Window light or warm lamp.
- **Style:** Golden, inviting. Not dramatic.

### Audio
- **Mic:** Lapel mic. Clean audio.
- **Environment:** Quiet room.

### Editing
- **Captions:** Hardcoded, bold white with dark shadow. Full transcript.
- **Text overlays:** Gentle fade in. "Nobody needs to know" and "'What are you doing?'" are the two key overlays -- hold each 3-4 seconds.
- **Cuts:** One or two max. This benefits from single-take authenticity.
- **Color grade:** Warm, golden, soft. Inviting. The visual equivalent of a quiet win.
- **Music:** Very soft acoustic guitar or piano. Warm, light. Not sentimental -- pleasant. Like background music at a coffee shop.
- **Pacing:** Relaxed. Medium tempo. Not rushed. The whole ad should feel easy, like the product it's describing.
- **End frame:** CTA overlay holds 3 seconds.
