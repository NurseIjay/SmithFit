## Photo/Image Direction
- **Text-only design.** The progression from bold headline to whisper-quiet details to the gold payoff quote creates a visual story arc. The muted details (#B0B0B0) feel like things she's NOT doing -- the absence of effort. The gold quote (#C5A55A) is the result of that absence. Elegant, minimal.
- **Optional:** Very faint (8% opacity) photo of morning light through a window as the background. Adds warmth, keeps it soft.
