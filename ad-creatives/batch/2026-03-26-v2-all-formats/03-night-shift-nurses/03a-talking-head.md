## Production Notes

### Setting
- **Option 1 (preferred):** Dark room with a single warm light source -- mimics the night-shift vibe. Like IJ is talking to you during a break.
- **Option 2:** Standard clean background with natural light.

### Wardrobe
- Scrubs (dark navy or charcoal). Badge lanyard visible. Stethoscope draped over shoulders optional.

### Camera
- iPhone on tripod. Vertical 9:16. Chest-up. Eye level. 2-3 feet distance.

### Lighting
- **For night-shift vibe (Option 1):** Single warm lamp to one side. Slightly moody, not dark. Face clearly visible.
- **For standard:** Natural window light, warm.

### Audio
- Lapel mic. Quiet room. No echo.

### Editing
- **Captions:** Hardcoded, full transcript. Bold white with dark shadow.
- **Text overlays:** As noted. Top-center. 2-3 seconds each.
- **Cuts:** Minimal. 1-2 jump cuts to tighten pacing. The straight-to-camera delivery is the strength.
- **Color grade:** Warm. Slightly cooler if using the night-shift-vibe setting.
- **Music:** None or very subtle ambient. This is a direct address -- let the words carry it.
