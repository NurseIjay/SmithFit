# Concept 03: Night Shift Nurses -- B-Roll
**Funnel Stage:** TOF (Cold)
**Format:** B-Roll with IJ Voiceover
**Duration:** 30-34 seconds
**Objective:** Night-shift-specific visuals with identity-driven voiceover, comparison shame, break room detail

---

## Shot List

### SHOT 1 -- Hospital Parking Lot at Night (Hook)
- **What to film:** Wide shot of a hospital parking lot at night. Streetlights casting pools of light. A single figure in scrubs walks toward a car in the distance. Quiet, isolated feeling.
- **Camera angle:** Wide, slightly low angle. Stationary camera.
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Streetlights, parking lot ambient. Cool blue tone. Some warm light spill from building entrance in background.
- **Props:** Scrubs, car in distance. Hospital or large building visible.

### SHOT 2 -- Badge Swipe / Clock-In
- **What to film:** Close-up of a hand swiping a badge across a card reader. The badge is visible -- shows a lanyard with "RN" or a nursing credential. The hand is purposeful but tired.
- **Camera angle:** Close-up, 45-degree angle from the side
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Lighting:** Fluorescent overhead (institutional feel)
- **Props:** Badge with lanyard, card reader (or mock one -- a doorframe works)

### SHOT 3 -- Clock Showing Night Hours
- **What to film:** Close-up of a phone lock screen or wall clock showing 2:17 AM. A coffee cup sits in the foreground, slightly out of focus.
- **Camera angle:** Close-up, shallow depth of field -- clock sharp, coffee soft
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Lighting:** Dim. Phone glow or clock face as primary light.
- **Props:** Phone or wall clock, coffee cup

### SHOT 3B -- Break Room at Night (NEW)
- **What to film:** Overhead shot of a break room table: an open pizza box with 2 slices left, a vending machine bag of chips, a half-empty coffee, a badge lanyard, and a phone. The sad reality of break room eating at 2 AM.
- **Camera angle:** Overhead bird's eye
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 2.5 seconds
- **Lighting:** Harsh fluorescent overhead. Unflattering on purpose.
- **Props:** Pizza box (open, 2 slices), chips bag, coffee cup, badge lanyard, phone, institutional table

### SHOT 4 -- Scrolling Other Nurses on Phone
- **What to film:** Close-up of a thumb scrolling through Instagram. The screen shows other nurses' content: meal prep photos, gym selfies in scrubs, "before/after" posts. Content that makes her feel behind.
- **Camera angle:** Over-the-shoulder, focused on phone screen
- **Movement:** Thumb scrolling slowly
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Phone screen glow in dim room
- **Props:** Phone with Instagram feed loaded (mock screenshots of nurse fitness content)

### SHOT 5 -- Post-Shift Couch Collapse
- **What to film:** Medium shot of someone in scrubs sitting on a couch, still in uniform, head back against the cushion, eyes closed. Shoes still on. A bag dropped on the floor beside the couch.
- **Camera angle:** Medium from the side, waist height
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 2.5 seconds
- **Lighting:** Low living room light. Curtains closed (blocking daylight for a night-shift worker sleeping during the day). Warm but dim.
- **Props:** Scrubs, couch, work bag on floor, shoes still on feet

### SHOT 6 -- IJ Writing on Whiteboard
- **What to film:** IJ's hand writing "SHIFT-BASED" on a whiteboard and circling it. Then crossing out "CLOCK-BASED" written above it.
- **Camera angle:** Close-up on the whiteboard, IJ's hand and marker visible
- **Movement:** Following the hand writing
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Clean, warm
- **Props:** Whiteboard, dry-erase marker (dark), "CLOCK-BASED" pre-written (or write both live)

### SHOT 7 -- Meal Template Cards
- **What to film:** Overhead shot of three simple cards/papers laid out on a clean counter: "WORK DAY" / "OFF DAY" / "CHAOS DAY." A hand slides the Chaos Day card forward, as if selecting it.
- **Camera angle:** Overhead bird's eye, tight on the three cards
- **Movement:** Static, then hand enters frame to slide card
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Bright, warm kitchen light -- contrast to the dark earlier shots
- **Props:** Three printed cards or handwritten labels, clean counter

### SHOT 8 -- Confident Walk-Out (Closing)
- **What to film:** IJ walking out of a door into daylight. Scrubs on, posture up, slight smile. The door opens and sunlight floods in. Hopeful, forward-looking.
- **Camera angle:** Medium from the front, slightly low angle (empowering)
- **Movement:** IJ walks toward camera, door opens behind him
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Bright daylight flooding through doorway -- lens flare acceptable. Warm.
- **Props:** Door (any -- house, building), scrubs, sunlight

## Complete Filming Guide

### Summary
- **Total shots:** 9
- **Estimated filming time:** 50-65 minutes
- **Locations needed:** 3 (parking lot/exterior, home interior, kitchen)

### Props Checklist
- [ ] Scrubs (dark navy/charcoal)
- [ ] Badge lanyard with ID
- [ ] Car keys
- [ ] Card reader (or mock doorframe)
- [ ] Phone showing 2:17 AM
- [ ] Coffee cup
- [ ] Pizza box (open, 2 slices left) + chips + water bottle
- [ ] Institutional-looking table/surface
- [ ] Phone with Instagram loaded (nurse fitness content)
- [ ] Couch + work bag
- [ ] Whiteboard + dry-erase marker
- [ ] Three printed cards: "WORK DAY" / "OFF DAY" / "CHAOS DAY"
- [ ] Door to exterior (for walk-out shot)

### Editing Notes
- **Cut pacing:** Shots 1-5 are the "problem" section -- medium pacing, 2-3 sec each. Building mood. Shots 6-8 are the "solution" -- slightly faster, brighter.
- **Color grade:** Shots 1-5: cool, slightly blue/green, desaturated (night shift vibe). Shots 6-8: warm, bright, saturated (the solution/hope).
- **The transition point:** Between Shot 5 and Shot 6 -- color shifts from cool to warm. Brief beat of silence or music shift.
- **Captions:** Full hardcoded. Bold white, lower third.
- **Music:** Low, ambient. Slightly eerie/tense for Shots 1-5. Warmer for 6-8. Think "documentary" not "workout."
