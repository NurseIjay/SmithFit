# Concept 13: I Know What You're Thinking -- B-Roll
**Funnel Stage:** BOF (Retargeting)
**Format:** B-Roll with IJ Voiceover
**Duration:** 26-30 seconds
**Objective:** Anti-ad visual approach that contrasts generic fitness culture with IJ's nurse reality

---

## Shot List

### SHOT 1 -- Scrolling Past Fitness Ads (Hook)
- **What to film:** Close-up of a thumb quickly swiping through Instagram stories. Each story is a different fitness ad -- abs, green smoothies, "TRANSFORM YOUR BODY" text, a countdown timer deal. She's scrolling past all of them. Fast, dismissive swipes.
- **Camera angle:** Over-the-shoulder tight on phone screen
- **Movement:** Static camera, thumb swiping rapidly
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Phone screen glow in a dim room. Post-shift scrolling.
- **Props:** Phone with mock Instagram stories loaded (generic fitness content)

### SHOT 2 -- IJ's Badge Close-Up
- **What to film:** Close-up of IJ's RN badge on a lanyard. The camera focuses on the credential -- "RN" clearly visible. The badge rests on scrubs. This is the visual proof.
- **Camera angle:** Tight close-up, slight angle, shallow depth of field
- **Movement:** Slow rack focus from blurred to sharp on the badge
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Lighting:** Natural, clean
- **Props:** RN badge, lanyard, scrubs

### SHOT 3 -- IJ in Scrubs, Not a Gym
- **What to film:** Medium shot of IJ in scrubs at his kitchen counter. Not posing. Not flexing. Making coffee or reading something on his phone. Normal. Anti-aspirational. Real.
- **Camera angle:** Medium from across the kitchen, candid feel
- **Movement:** Static, natural handheld drift
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Warm kitchen light. Morning.
- **Props:** Scrubs, coffee mug, kitchen counter, phone

### SHOT 4 -- Hand Writing "$27" on Paper
- **What to film:** Close-up of a hand writing "$27" on a notepad. Below it, writing "60-day guarantee." Simple. No flash. Just the facts on paper.
- **Camera angle:** Overhead tight on paper and hand
- **Movement:** Following the pen
- **Duration:** 2.5 seconds
- **Lighting:** Warm desk lamp
- **Props:** Notepad, pen

### SHOT 5 -- Phone Showing Community Messages
- **What to film:** Close-up of a phone screen showing real-looking community messages: "Day 3 and my energy is already different," "My scrubs fit different this week," "I can't believe this is working." Thumb scrolling slowly through them.
- **Camera angle:** Over-the-shoulder on phone screen
- **Movement:** Slow scroll
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Natural ambient
- **Props:** Phone with community message screenshots (can be mocked up)

### SHOT 6 -- IJ to Camera (The Bridge)
- **What to film:** IJ speaking to camera. Scrubs. Calm, confident, slightly self-aware smile. This is the face moment -- the skepticism buster resolves into a real person.
- **Camera angle:** Chest-up, eye level, straight-on, close (1.5-2 feet)
- **Movement:** Static (tripod)
- **Duration:** 5 seconds
- **Lighting:** Natural window light, warm
- **Props:** Scrubs, badge visible, clean background

### SHOT 7 -- Metabolic Cocktail Prep
- **What to film:** Close-up of hands mixing the metabolic cocktail -- water, lemon, salt. Simple, calm, intentional. The action itself says "this isn't complicated."
- **Camera angle:** Medium close-up, 45-degree angle
- **Movement:** Slight handheld drift
- **Duration:** 2.5 seconds
- **Lighting:** Warm morning light through window
- **Props:** Clear glass, lemon, salt, clean counter

### SHOT 8 -- Closing Shot: IJ Walking Away from Camera
- **What to film:** Medium-wide of IJ walking away from camera down a hallway or path. Casual. Scrubs or clean clothes. Not dramatic -- just a guy heading off. The anti-ad close. He said his piece.
- **Camera angle:** Medium-wide from behind, stationary
- **Movement:** Static, IJ walks away
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Warm natural light
- **Props:** Casual clothes or scrubs

## Complete Filming Guide

### Summary
- **Total shots:** 8
- **Estimated filming time:** 35-45 minutes
- **Locations needed:** 2 (home interior, hallway/outdoor)
- **Wardrobe:** Scrubs with badge

### Props Checklist
- [ ] Phone with mock Instagram fitness ads loaded
- [ ] RN badge and lanyard
- [ ] Scrubs (dark navy)
- [ ] Coffee mug
- [ ] Notepad and pen
- [ ] Phone with community message screenshots
- [ ] Clear glass, lemon, salt
- [ ] Casual clothes (optional for walk-away shot)

### Location Checklist
- [ ] Kitchen (IJ candid shot, cocktail prep)
- [ ] Desk area (writing shot)
- [ ] Clean background room (IJ to camera)
- [ ] Hallway or outdoor path (walk-away shot)

### Editing Notes
- **Cut pacing:** Slightly faster than average. This ad has momentum. Shots 1-3 are quick setup. Shots 4-5 are the proof section -- medium pace. Shot 6 (IJ to camera) lingers -- this is the trust moment. Shots 7-8 close efficiently.
- **Color grade:** Neutral throughout. No heavy color work. The lack of grading IS the aesthetic -- it says "this isn't produced, this is real." Slight warmth on Shot 6 (IJ to camera) only.
- **Captions:** Full hardcoded. Bold white, lower third.
- **Music:** None. This is the anti-ad. Silence and voice only. If there's any ambient sound (coffee mug, writing) that's fine -- it adds reality.
- **The contrast:** Shot 1 (flashy fitness ads) vs. Shot 3 (IJ making coffee in scrubs) should feel like a visual argument. The juxtaposition IS the message.
