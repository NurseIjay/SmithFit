# Concept 13: I Know What You're Thinking -- Talking Head
**Funnel Stage:** BOF (Retargeting)
**Format:** Talking Head (IJ to camera)
**Duration:** 25-32 seconds
**Objective:** Name the skepticism head-on, differentiate from every other fitness ad, build trust for the retargeting audience who saw a previous ad but didn't convert

---

## Script (Word-for-Word)

### HOOK (0-4 sec)
**IJ says:** "I know. Another fitness ad. Another coach. Another promise. But let me ask you something -- how many of those coaches are nurses?"

**On-screen text overlay:** "How many of those coaches are nurses?"
**IJ's energy/expression:** Slight smirk at the start. He knows this is going to sound like every other ad. The "I know" is delivered with self-awareness, almost amused. Then "how many of those coaches are nurses?" -- lean in, direct eye contact, dead serious. The shift from knowing to serious is the creative.

---

### DIFFERENTIATION (4-12 sec)
**IJ says:** "Look. I get it. You've been burned. You've seen a hundred of these. Some coach with abs telling you to wake up at 5 AM and meal prep your life away. That's not me. I'm an RN. I work shifts. I built this because nothing else worked for me."

**On-screen text overlay:** "I'm an RN. I work shifts." (appears at 8 sec)
**IJ's energy/expression:** This is a conversation, not a pitch. "I get it" is genuine acknowledgment. "Some coach with abs" -- slight eye roll, he's on her side here. "I'm an RN" -- simple, factual, no flexing. "Nothing else worked for me" -- real. This is where the trust is built.

---

### PROOF (12-20 sec)
**IJ says:** "I'm not going to promise you a magic pill. I'm going to tell you what I tell every nurse I work with. Your cortisol from shift work has been running your metabolism into the ground. And until you address that, nothing else is going to stick. Four hundred nurses have done this. Most of them felt different by Day 3."

**On-screen text overlay:** "400 nurses. Most felt different by Day 3."
**IJ's energy/expression:** Calm authority. Not hype. Not excitement. The energy of a nurse explaining something to a patient -- clear, steady, honest. "Magic pill" is tossed off casually. The cortisol explanation is matter-of-fact. "Four hundred nurses" lands with quiet confidence.

---

### CTA (20-28 sec)
**IJ says:** "It's $27. If it doesn't work, you get your money back. 60-day guarantee. The only risk is scrolling past this and doing nothing. Link in bio."

**On-screen text overlay:** "$27 | 60-day guarantee | The only risk is scrolling past this."
**IJ's energy/expression:** Direct but not aggressive. "The only risk is scrolling past this" is delivered with a slight pause before "scrolling past this" -- he's naming what she's about to do and giving her a reason not to. Brief nod at the end. Holds eye contact through the close.

## Production Notes

### Setting
- **Location:** Simple, clean room. Could be a home office, living room, or kitchen.
- **Background:** Neutral. Nothing shiny, nothing aspirational. This is meant to feel anti-ad.
- **Vibe:** A guy who knows you're skeptical and isn't offended by it. He respects the skepticism.

### Wardrobe
- **Option 1 (preferred):** Scrubs with badge. The visual proof of the credential.
- **Option 2:** Clean casual tee. The verbal delivery establishes the RN identity clearly enough.

### Camera
- **Device:** iPhone on tripod
- **Framing:** Chest-up, centered, vertical (9:16 / 1080x1920)
- **Angle:** Eye level. Slightly closer than standard -- 2 feet. Personal.

### Lighting
- **Primary:** Natural window light. Clean, honest.
- **Avoid:** Anything that looks "produced." The anti-ad feeling extends to the visuals.

### Audio
- **Mic:** Lapel mic. Clean, clear audio. No echo.
- **Environment:** Quiet.

### Editing
- **Captions:** Hardcoded, bold white with dark shadow. Full transcript.
- **Text overlays:** Standard timing, snap in. The key overlay is "The only risk is scrolling past this" -- hold that one 4 seconds.
- **Cuts:** One or two tight jump cuts. This ad should feel slightly raw, like a single take that got tightened. Not polished.
- **Color grade:** Neutral. Not warm, not cool. Honest. Minimal grading. Let the raw quality signal authenticity.
- **Music:** None. Zero. The anti-ad quality means no music. His voice is it.
- **Pacing:** Conversational. Medium tempo. He's not rushing, he's not slow-playing. He's talking to someone he respects.
- **End frame:** CTA overlay holds 3 seconds.
