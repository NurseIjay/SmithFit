## Photo/Image Direction
- **Text-only design.** The muted "another ad" lines fading into the bold "nurses?" question IS the visual trick. The contrast between the gray (what she's tired of seeing) and the gold/cream (what's different) creates the stop.
- **Optional:** Very faint (5% opacity) RN badge watermark behind the text. Subtle credential signal.
