# Concept 11: Who Takes Care of You? -- Static Image
**Funnel Stage:** TOF (Cold)
**Format:** Static Image Ad
**Objective:** Emotional scroll-stopper that names the caregiver identity, drives engagement or click

## Photo/Image Direction
- **Text-only design.** The repetitive structure ("You take care of...") building to the gold question IS the visual. The pattern-then-break creates the scroll stop.
- **Optional:** Very faint (8% opacity) warm-toned photo of hands folded in a lap as full background. Adds human warmth without distraction.

## A/B Variations

### Variation A (Default -- repetitive build to question)
- Four "you take care of" lines building to "who takes care of you?"
- Pattern-interrupt through repetition

### Variation B (Single powerful question)
- Background: #2D2D2D (dark)
- Single centered line: "Who takes care of you?" -- Serif, 72px, #FFF8F0, "you" in #C5A55A italic
- Below: "The Shift-Proof Metabolism Reset. Built by a nurse. For nurses." -- 24px, #B0B0B0
- CTA: Gold bar with "$27 | Link in bio" -- #C5A55A background, white text
- Minimal, dramatic, all negative space
- Tests whether the question alone stops the scroll without setup
