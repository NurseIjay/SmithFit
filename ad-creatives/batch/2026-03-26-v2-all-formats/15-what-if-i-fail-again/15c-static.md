## Photo/Image Direction
- **Text-only design.** The emotional arc is in the typography: muted fear (#B0B0B0) at the top, transitioning through the validation (#4A4A4A) to the bold gold proof points (#C5A55A). The visual hierarchy mirrors the emotional journey from doubt to safety.
- **No photos.** The vulnerability of the question "what if I fail again?" is stronger without a face attached -- it stays internal, personal.

## A/B Variations

### Variation A (Default -- cream, fear to safety)
- Muted fear question, gold proof points, warm
- Emotional arc in the typography

### Variation B (Dark, stark)
- Background: #2D2D2D
- "What if I fail again?" -- Serif italic, 48px, #B0B0B0 at 60% (barely visible -- like a whisper)
- "Your fear makes sense." -- 28px, #FFF8F0
- "87%" and "60 days" in #C5A55A (gold pops against dark)
- "Worst case: money back. Best case: everything changes." -- #FFF8F0
- CTA bar: Gold on dark
- More dramatic, nighttime-scrolling energy
- Tests whether the vulnerability hits harder in dark or light
