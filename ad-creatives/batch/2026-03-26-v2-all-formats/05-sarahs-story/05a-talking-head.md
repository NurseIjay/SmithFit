# Concept 05: Sarah's Story -- Talking Head
**Funnel Stage:** MOF (Warm)
**Format:** Talking Head (IJ telling Sarah's story to camera)
**Duration:** 34-42 seconds
**Objective:** Client transformation with "not the exception" language, quiet transformation, FOMO

---

## Script (Word-for-Word)

### HOOK (0-4 sec)
**IJ says:** "Let me tell you about Sarah. She's 38. She's a nurse. She has 3 kids and she's in the process of adopting her 4th."

**On-screen text overlay:** "Sarah. 38. Nurse. 3 kids. Adopting her 4th."
**IJ's energy/expression:** Setting the scene. Warm storyteller energy. Eye contact. Slight lean in -- "let me tell you" is conspiratorial, like sharing something special.

---

### PROBLEM (4-12 sec)
**IJ says:** "She came to me exhausted. She'd tried 1,000-calorie diets. She was eating less and less and nothing was changing. Her body felt like it had given up on her. And can I be honest? Sarah thought she was the one person this wouldn't work for. She almost didn't start."

**On-screen text overlay:** "1,000-calorie diets. Nothing was changing." (at 5 sec) then "She almost didn't start." (at 10 sec)
**IJ's energy/expression:** Empathy. Slow down on "nothing was changing." Slight headshake. "She thought she was the one person" -- look right at the camera. The viewer IS Sarah right now.

---

### MECHANISM (12-20 sec)
**IJ says:** "Here's what we figured out: her stress hormones were through the roof from years of shift work. Her body was in survival mode. Eating less was actually making her cortisol worse. She needed to calm her nervous system down first."

**On-screen text overlay:** "Her cortisol was through the roof." (at 13 sec) then "Eating less made it WORSE." (at 17 sec)
**IJ's energy/expression:** Diagnostic energy -- IJ the RN explaining what he found. Confident, clear. "Eating less made it worse" -- this is the counterintuitive hook. Lean in. Let it land.

---

### PROOF (20-32 sec)
**IJ says:** "So she started the reset. And she started eating MORE. Not less -- more. She lost 30 lbs in 8 and a half weeks. Her resting heart rate dropped 12 to 15 beats per minute. She didn't announce a new diet at work. She didn't post about it. People at work started asking what changed. And she told me: 'I feel like myself again.' She's not doing anything extreme. She's not meal prepping for hours. She looks and feels like herself -- and other nurses noticed."

**On-screen text overlay:** "Lost 30 lbs eating MORE. In 8.5 weeks." (at 22 sec) then "Resting HR down 12-15 BPM" (at 26 sec) then "'I feel like myself again.'" (at 29 sec)
**IJ's energy/expression:** Building excitement. "Eating MORE" -- pause for impact. Genuine pride. Slow down on "I feel like myself again." The quiet transformation section -- "she didn't announce a new diet" -- should feel understated, private. This is the stealth result the viewer wants.

---

### CTA (32-40 sec)
**IJ says:** "Sarah wasn't the exception. She thought she was. She wasn't. Neither are you. 400 nurses have done this. 87% feel a difference by Day 3. If Sarah can do it with 3 kids, 12-hour shifts, and a new adoption -- you can too. Link in bio. It's $27."

**On-screen text overlay:** "You're not the exception. | $27 | 60-day guarantee | Link in bio"
**IJ's energy/expression:** Bridge from Sarah to the viewer. "Neither are you" -- direct, firm. "You can too" -- direct eye contact, confident warmth. Not salesy -- inviting.

## Production Notes

### Setting
- Home, simple background. Warm lighting. Same setup as Concept 01 talking head.

### Wardrobe
- Scrubs or clean casual. Either works -- this is a story-telling piece. Badge optional.

### Camera
- iPhone on tripod. Vertical 9:16. Chest-up. Eye level.

### Lighting
- Natural window light, warm. Consistent with other talking head ads.

### Audio
- Lapel mic. Quiet room.

### Editing
- **Captions:** Hardcoded, full transcript. Bold white, dark shadow.
- **Text overlays:** As noted. Key stats get larger text treatment.
- **Cuts:** 2-3 jump cuts OK to tighten. The story arc should flow naturally.
- **Color grade:** Warm throughout. This is a hopeful story -- no cool/dark sections.
- **Music:** Very subtle, warm ambient. Comes in softly around the proof section (20 sec). Never competes with voice.
- **Key moment:** On "I feel like myself again" -- brief pause, let the quote hang for 1.5 seconds. This is the emotional climax.
- **Compliance:** "Results may vary" text overlay appears with the weight loss stat.
