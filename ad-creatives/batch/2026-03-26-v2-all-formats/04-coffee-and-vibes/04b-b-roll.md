# Concept 04: Coffee and Vibes -- B-Roll
**Funnel Stage:** TOF (Cold)
**Format:** B-Roll with IJ Voiceover
**Duration:** 22-26 seconds
**Objective:** Quick, warm, relatable B-roll with break room detail, avatar quote, identity shift

---

## Shot List

### SHOT 1 -- Coffee Pour (Hook)
- **What to film:** Close-up of coffee being poured into a travel mug. Steam curls up. The travel mug has a nursing-related sticker or is generic hospital-style. A badge lanyard sits on the counter beside it.
- **Camera angle:** 45-degree close-up, focused on the pour
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Lighting:** Early morning or late afternoon kitchen light. Warm, golden.
- **Props:** Coffee pot, travel mug, badge lanyard, kitchen counter

### SHOT 2 -- Break Room Pizza (NEW)
- **What to film:** Overhead shot of a break room table: an open pizza box with a couple slices left, a coffee cup, a badge lanyard. A hand reaches in and grabs a slice. This is the "only thing you've eaten since 7 PM" moment.
- **Camera angle:** Overhead bird's eye
- **Movement:** Static, hand enters frame
- **Duration:** 2.5 seconds
- **Lighting:** Harsh fluorescent overhead (institutional, unflattering)
- **Props:** Pizza box (open), coffee cup, badge lanyard, institutional table

### SHOT 3 -- Rings on Fingers
- **What to film:** Extreme close-up of a woman's hand (or IJ's hand if demonstrating) twisting a ring on a swollen finger. The ring is tight. The gesture is small but universally recognized.
- **Camera angle:** Extreme close-up, eye level with the hand
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Lighting:** Natural ambient, sharp focus
- **Props:** Ring on finger (wedding band or simple band)

### SHOT 4 -- Scrubs Pull
- **What to film:** Medium shot from the waist down: hands pulling at the waistband of scrub pants, adjusting them. Not dramatic -- the casual, habitual gesture of clothes not fitting right.
- **Camera angle:** Medium from the front, waist-down crop
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Lighting:** Bedroom or bathroom natural light
- **Props:** Scrub pants

### SHOT 5 -- Phone Glow in Dark Room
- **What to film:** A phone screen glowing in a dark room. The screen shows social media (Instagram explore page with fitness content). A tired hand holds the phone. Scrub sleeve barely visible.
- **Camera angle:** Medium from above, looking down at the phone and the person's lap
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Lighting:** Phone screen is the only light source. Everything else is dark.
- **Props:** Phone with Instagram loaded, scrub sleeve, dark room

### SHOT 6 -- IJ to Camera (Validation)
- **What to film:** IJ looking at camera with warm empathy. Mouth forms the beginning of "It's not you" -- then cut. This is a quick, connecting moment.
- **Camera angle:** Chest-up, eye level, close
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Lighting:** Warm, natural
- **Props:** Casual clothing, clean background

### SHOT 7 -- Morning Glass of Water
- **What to film:** Close-up of a clear glass of water with a lemon wedge being placed on a clean, sunlit counter. Simple, calm, intentional. The "reset" visual.
- **Camera angle:** 45-degree close-up
- **Movement:** Slight push-in
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Lighting:** Bright morning sunlight
- **Props:** Clear glass, water, lemon wedge, clean counter

### SHOT 8 -- Walking into Shift with Energy (NEW)
- **What to film:** Medium shot of someone in well-fitting scrubs walking with good posture through a doorway or down a hallway. Confident step. Not looking tired. The "imagine walking into shift with energy" visual.
- **Camera angle:** Medium from the front, slightly low angle (empowering)
- **Movement:** Static, person walks toward camera
- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Lighting:** Bright, warm natural light
- **Props:** Well-fitting scrubs, badge lanyard

### SHOT 9 -- CTA Moment
- **What to film:** IJ looking at camera, slight confident nod. Holds eye contact. Warmth.
- **Camera angle:** Chest-up, eye level
- **Movement:** Static
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Lighting:** Warm, natural
- **Props:** Casual or scrubs. Clean background.

## Complete Filming Guide

### Summary
- **Total shots:** 9
- **Estimated filming time:** 30-35 minutes
- **Locations needed:** 2 (home kitchen/bedroom, break room or mock break room area)

### Props Checklist
- [ ] Coffee pot and travel mug
- [ ] Badge lanyard
- [ ] Pizza box (open, a few slices) + coffee cup
- [ ] Institutional-looking table/surface
- [ ] Ring (simple band)
- [ ] Scrub pants
- [ ] Phone with Instagram loaded
- [ ] Clear glass + lemon wedge
- [ ] Well-fitting scrubs for Shot 8
- [ ] Casual clothing for IJ (Shots 6, 9)

### Editing Notes
- **Cut pacing:** Fast. 2 seconds per shot for Shots 1-5. This builds the "day in the life" montage feel. Shots 6-9 slow down slightly for the resolution.
- **Color grade:** Shots 1-5: natural, slightly cool/desaturated (reality). Shots 6-9: warmer, brighter (hope).
- **Captions:** Full hardcoded. Bold white, lower third.
- **Music:** Light, slightly upbeat ambient. Not gym music -- think "lo-fi study beats" energy. Subtle. Cuts out at Shot 6 (the validation moment) for emphasis, then returns softly.
- **Feel:** This should feel like a 22-second TikTok that's been filmed casually. Not polished. Real.
