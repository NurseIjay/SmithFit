2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To provide the courses, membership content, coaching, meal plans, workout programs, and community access you purchased.
  • Program personalization: To customize nutrition guidance, workout recommendations, and coaching based on your health data, shift schedule, and goals.
  • Communication: To send you onboarding emails, check-in reminders, coaching call invitations, membership updates, and responses to your inquiries.
  • Marketing: To send you promotional emails about SmithFit products and services. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email.
  • Billing: To process payments, manage subscriptions, and handle refund requests.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and feedback to improve our programs and services.
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, respond to legal requests, and protect our rights.

4. Health Information

Important: SmithFit collects health-related information (weight, body measurements, dietary habits, sleep patterns, stress levels, and progress photos) solely for the purpose of delivering and personalizing our wellness programs. SmithFit is not a healthcare provider and is not subject to HIPAA. However, we treat your health information with the same care and confidentiality we would expect for our own.

Your health information is:

  • Used only for program delivery and personalization
  • Accessible only to SmithFit coaching staff and authorized service providers
  • Never sold to third parties
  • Never used for insurance, employment, or any purpose unrelated to your SmithFit program

5. Progress Photos and Testimonials

If you submit progress photos through the SmithFit app or check-in process:

  • Photos are stored securely within our platform providers (Trainerize, FitMetrics).
  • We may request to use your photos and/or testimonials for marketing purposes (social media, website, advertising).
  • We will not publicly share your photos without your consent.
  • You may withdraw consent and request removal of any publicly shared photos at any time by emailing coaches@smithfit.co with the subject "Photo Privacy Request."

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for website functionality, login sessions, and payment processing. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve the experience. These are provided by GoHighLevel's built-in analytics.
  • Marketing cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertising and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns (e.g., Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics if enabled).

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active and you are using our services.
  • After cancellation: We retain your account data and progress history for 90 days after cancellation, in case you choose to rejoin. After 90 days, we will delete or anonymize your data upon request.
  • Payment records: Transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by tax and accounting regulations.
  • Email communications: Marketing email history is retained until you unsubscribe, at which point your email is added to our suppression list to prevent future contact.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain records.
  • Opt-out of marketing: You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in any email, or by emailing coaches@smithfit.co.
  • Photo removal: You may request removal of any photos used for marketing at any time.
  • Data portability: You may request your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email coaches@smithfit.co with the subject line "Privacy Request." We will respond within 30 days.

9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), including the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

SmithFit does not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

To submit a CCPA request, email coaches@smithfit.co with "California Privacy Request" in the subject line. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information. These include:

  • Encrypted payment processing through Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1)
  • Secure, password-protected access to our platforms
  • Limited access to personal data (only SmithFit coaching staff and authorized personnel)
  • Regular review of our data handling practices

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If we become aware of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.

11. Children's Privacy

SmithFit services are intended for individuals 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email at least 14 days before taking effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.
