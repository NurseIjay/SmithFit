What's Working (Keep)

STRENGTH

Empathy opening copy

"Hey. I Get It." — the opening letter reads like a real person talking to someone who's been burned. Validates the prospect's skepticism before asking for anything. Keep this tone.

STRENGTH

14-Day Timeline concept

Day 1 → Day 14 milestone progression makes the trial feel like a journey, not just a free period. Each step builds anticipation. The 94% retention stat at Day 14 is a strong close.

STRENGTH

Credit card objection handling

Addressing "Why do I need a credit card?" head-on with honest commitment psychology framing is above average for downsell pages.

STRENGTH

"You Have a Simple Choice" comparison

Two-column Leave vs. Start format at the bottom is solid closing psychology. Clear contrast between inaction and action.
