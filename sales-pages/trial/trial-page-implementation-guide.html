Free Trial Downsell — Implementation Guide

Step-by-step instructions for applying all fixes in GHL

DO FIRST — 5 min each

Step 1: Replace blank hero

The top of the page is completely empty. This is the #1 bounce cause.

  1. Open the free trial page in GHL page editor
  2. Find the empty section at the very top (before "A Personal Note")
  3. Delete the blank section
  4. Add a new Custom HTML/Code element at the top
  5. Paste the contents of trial-artifact-hero.html
  6. Save and preview — you should see the headline and green CTA button
DO FIRST — 5 min

Step 2: Fix or remove broken video

  1. Find the video section (between Sarah K. testimonial and the timeline)
  2. Option A: Upload a working video to GHL media library → update the video element source
  3. Option B: Replace with a Loom embed if you have one recorded
  4. Option C: Delete the entire video section if no video exists yet (better to have no video than a broken one)
DO FIRST — 5 min

Step 3: Replace blank feature cards

  1. Find the "FULL ACCESS — Not a Trial Version" section with 6 blank white cards
  2. Delete all 6 cards and the section container
  3. Add a new Custom HTML/Code element in its place
  4. Paste the contents of trial-artifact-feature-cards.html
  5. Preview — you should see 6 cards with emoji icons and descriptions
DO FIRST — 2 min

Step 4: Fix all broken links

  1. Footer links: Update Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Contact to real SmithFit URLs (or # if pages don't exist yet)
  2. "No thanks" link: Change from manus.space to either your $27 course page URL or simply #
  3. Search the page for "manus.space" — replace ALL instances
DO FIRST — 5 min

Step 5: Replace fake pricing table

  1. Find the pricing table section (shows ~~$349/mo~~ → $67/mo)
  2. Delete the entire pricing table
  3. Add a Custom HTML/Code element
  4. Paste the contents of trial-artifact-pricing-card.html
  5. Verify the CTA button links to your GHL checkout/trial signup URL
DO NEXT — 5 min each

Step 6: Replace closing photo

  1. Find the closing section with "Let's See What Your Body Can Do" and the male before/after photo
  2. Replace the image with a photo of IJ in scrubs, coaching context, or a friendly headshot
  3. Do NOT use a shirtless transformation photo — the page audience is female nurses
Note: Same fix applied to membership and bundle pages. Use the same replacement photo across all 3 for brand consistency.
DO NEXT — 5 min

Step 7: Add expanded FAQ section

  1. Find the "Why Do I Need to Enter a Credit Card" single-question section
  2. Delete it
  3. Add a Custom HTML/Code element
  4. Paste the contents of trial-artifact-objection-faq.html
  5. This replaces the single question with a full 6-question accordion that also covers the "Canceling Is Simple" steps
DO NEXT — 5 min

Step 8: Upgrade the timeline

  1. Find the current 14-day timeline section
  2. Replace with trial-artifact-timeline.html for the animated version
  3. OR keep the current timeline if you prefer — it's functional, just less engaging
DO LAST — 3 min

Step 9: Add sticky CTA

  1. Add a Custom HTML/Code element at the very bottom of the page (after footer)
  2. Paste the contents of trial-artifact-sticky-cta.html
  3. This creates a floating bar that appears after scrolling — no layout changes needed
  4. Update the CTA link URL to your trial signup checkout
DO LAST — 5 min

Step 10: Verify all CTA buttons link correctly

  1. Click through every green "YES — Start My 14-Day FREE Trial →" button
  2. Confirm they all go to the correct GHL checkout/trial signup page
  3. Test on both desktop and mobile
  4. Verify the sticky CTA button also links correctly
