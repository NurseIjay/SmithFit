## SCRIPT 1: Trainerize App Welcome Message

### Full Version (under 150 words)

Welcome to SmithFit.

You just did something most nurses never do — you chose yourself. That matters more than you know right now.

Here's what's inside your app:

- **Meal Plans** — Work Day, Off Day, and Chaos Day templates (because not every shift goes to plan)
- **1,000+ Recipes** — searchable, filterable, built for real schedules
- **15-Minute Workouts** — cortisol-conscious, 3 levels, no gym required
- **Progress Tracking** — so you can see what's shifting, even when the scale doesn't tell the whole story

This app isn't here to punish you. It's built for nurses who work 12s, sleep weird hours, and still want to feel like themselves again.

**Your first step:** Open the Meal Plans tab and find your shift type — Work Day, Off Day, or Chaos Day. Pick whichever matches tomorrow. That's it. You're already ahead.

You belong here. Let's go.

— IJ
