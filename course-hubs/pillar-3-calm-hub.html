Morning Metabolic Reset Cocktail
2 minutes. 4 ingredients. The first safety signal of your day.
Base Recipe
8-12 oz water
½ tsp quality salt
½ fresh lemon (juice)
Optional Additions
¼ tsp cream of tartar (potassium)
200mg magnesium glycinate
3 Variations
Sparkling: Use sparkling water instead
Warm: Use warm water (soothing for night shift wake-ups)
Flavor-Infused: Add a splash of 100% cranberry juice or apple cider vinegar
Timing
First thing after waking, BEFORE coffee. This starts your day with a hydration and electrolyte signal, not a stimulant signal.
If you have kidney issues or are on sodium-restricted medications, check with your healthcare provider before adding extra salt.

If This → Then That: Calm Adjustments

Click any scenario to see the adjustment. These are your emergency protocols when life disrupts your calm routine.

😫 I woke up puffy and inflamed →
Cocktail + extra water + protein anchor. Skip the coffee until you've had 16oz water. Puffiness responds to hydration, not restriction.
😴 Can't fall asleep after night shift →
Full Sleep Switch (2-2-1). Dim lights immediately. No food within 30 min of sleep. If still awake after 20 min, get up and do the Brain Cue again.
😰 Wired/anxious before my shift →
2-Min SOS Reset in your car before walking in. Physiological sighs + grounding. "I am prepared. I can handle what comes."
🤕 Headache — probably dehydrated →
16oz water with electrolytes immediately. Cocktail if you haven't had it. Wait 20 min before taking anything else.
⏰ I missed my morning cocktail →
Have it whenever you remember. Better late than never. Even mid-shift with a water bottle + salt packet works.
☕ I drank coffee before my cocktail →
No guilt. Have the cocktail now. Tomorrow, set your ingredients out the night before so they're the first thing you see.
👨‍👩‍👧 Partner/kids disrupted my sleep →
This is a Yellow Day for calm. Do the 2-Min SOS. Lower expectations for the day. Protect your protein anchor and hydration.
🧠 Racing thoughts won't stop →
Brain dump exercise from Sleep Switch Step 3. Write everything down. Then do 3 rounds of physiological sighs. Your brain needs to externalize before it can rest.
🥤 Energy drink temptation →
Have a protein snack + water first. Wait 15 min. If you still want it, have it — but pair it with protein. No shame, just structure.
🔴 Period week — everything feels harder →
Increase magnesium to 400mg. Extra cocktail in the afternoon. Give yourself permission for Red Day pace. Hormones are real.
