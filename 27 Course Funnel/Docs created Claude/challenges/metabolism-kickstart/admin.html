Challenge Info

Name: 7-Day Metabolism Kickstart Challenge

Category: Hybrid — Nutrition + Movement + Recovery

Duration: 7 days (March 23 – March 29, 2026)

Audience: New $67/mo Metabolic Breakthrough Membership members

Challenge Goal

First-week activation sequence — get new members to complete four milestone actions that signal long-term retention:

  • Day 1: Make the metabolic cocktail — first physical action with the method
  • Day 2: Complete first workout — signal sent to the nervous system
  • Day 4: Post first win to the Wins Board — community investment begins
  • Day 7: Attend first group coaching call — live connection with IJ and the team

Members who hit all 4 milestones in Week 1 have significantly higher 90-day retention. This challenge is an activation protocol, not just a content event.

Why This Works

New members are most likely to ghost in Week 1. This challenge forces 7 light-lift daily actions that stack identity ("I'm someone who does this") before the initial excitement fades. Each day introduces one tool from the membership: cocktail, app workout, shift-proof nutrition, Wins Board, box breathing, FitMetrics AI, and the coaching call. By Day 7 they've touched every major deliverable once.

Team Assignments

  • VA: Post daily prompts in SmithFit FAM by 7:00 AM EST each day. Send reminder DMs to non-posters by Day 3.
  • IJ: React to every Wins Board post (Day 4). Host Day 7 coaching call. Send personal DMs to standout members.
  • Coach Sony: Monitor community daily. Engage in SmithFit FAM. Support Day 7 call as co-host.
  • Mom: Track daily poster participation in the Engagement tab checkbox grid. Flag non-participants to VA by each evening.
