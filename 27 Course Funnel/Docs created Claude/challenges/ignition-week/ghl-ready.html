Movement Challenge

5-Minute Ignition Week

"You don't need another 60-minute program. You need proof that you can still move your body — even when life gets messy."

Monday, March 23 – Sunday, March 29, 2026

Why This Matters

I've been where you are — running on 3 hours of sleep after a 12-hour shift, wondering if you even have the energy to take care of yourself. Here's what I know after years of coaching nurses, shift workers, and busy women: the hardest part isn't the workout. It's believing you deserve to show up for yourself.

This week, we're lighting a match. Small. Consistent. Powerful. By Sunday, you're going to feel the shift.

Show Up 5 of 7 Days

That's it. 5 posts in the community = challenge complete. You did the thing. You're stronger than the bad weeks.

How to Participate

Each day, pick the tier that matches your energy. Foundation is for your hardest days — and there's zero shame in staying there all week. Build is for when you've got a little more in the tank. Momentum is for the days you're feeling unstoppable.

Do the movement. Take the photo or video. Post it in the community with what tier you chose. That post is your proof — and your win.
