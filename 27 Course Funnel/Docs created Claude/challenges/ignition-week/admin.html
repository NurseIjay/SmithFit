Challenge Info

Name: 5-Minute Ignition Week

Category: Movement

Duration: 7 days (March 23 – March 29, 2026)

Completion: 5 of 7 days

Why This Matters (Admin Version)

Low-barrier daily movement challenge. Every member can do Foundation tier even on their worst day. Builds the habit of posting daily, which is our #1 retention lever. Movement challenges are the most popular category — high participation, great photo content for social proof.

Scheduling Notes

VA posts daily prompt in community by 7:00 AM EST each day. IJ or wife drops a comment on the prompt post by 9:00 AM to model engagement. Mom monitors community feed for participation and flags anyone who hasn't posted by Day 3.

Team Assignments

  • VA: Daily prompt posting (7 AM), graphic creation
  • IJ: Engagement comments (9 AM), Day 1 + Day 7 video messages
  • Wife: Workout demo support if needed
  • Mom: Participation monitoring, nudge DMs
⚠ Key Reminders: Challenge starts Monday March 23. All daily prompts should go live by 7 AM for maximum early engagement. IJ's 9 AM comment is crucial for signaling this is a priority.
Day 1 (Monday)
Light the Match

Your job is simple: move your body first thing. Whether it's a 5-minute walk or a 20-minute power session, you're sending a signal. You're here. You're moving.

Foundation: 5-minute walk around your block or house
Build: 10-minute brisk walk + 10 bodyweight squats
Momentum: 20-minute power walk or jog + 20 squats + 10 push-ups

Post: Selfie or photo from your walk/movement. Caption: "Match = lit 🔥"

Suggested posting time: 7:00 AM EST

Day 2 (Tuesday)
Strength Signal

Strength isn't about showing off. It's about reminding your nervous system that you're capable.

Foundation: 5 wall push-ups + 5 chair squats
Build: 10 push-ups + 15 squats + 10 lunges
Momentum: 3 rounds: 15 push-ups, 20 squats, 15 lunges, 10 glute bridges

Post: Quick video or photo of your favorite move. Caption: "Signal sent 💪"

Suggested posting time: 7:00 AM EST

Day 4 (Thursday)
The Comeback Walk

Walking isn't lazy. Walking is medicine. Walking is a superpower when you're busy, tired, or just done with complicated workouts.

Foundation: 5-minute walk (inside or outside)
Build: 15-minute walk at a pace that makes you breathe harder
Momentum: 30-minute walk with incline OR add 50 walking lunges

Post: Photo from your walk or step count. Caption: "Comebacks start with one step 👟"

Suggested posting time: 7:00 AM EST

Day 5 (Friday)
Friday Fire

You made it to Friday. Your nervous system survived another week. Now it's time to celebrate that with movement that feels GOOD.

Foundation: 5-minute dance party in your kitchen (pick a song, move your body)
Build: 15-minute at-home circuit (squats, push-ups, plank, repeat 3x)
Momentum: 25-minute full-body workout (add weights if you've got them)

Post: Video of your dance party OR post-workout selfie. Caption: "Friday FIRE 🔥"

Suggested posting time: 7:00 AM EST

Day 6 (Saturday)
Move With Joy

This is the day you remember that movement doesn't have to feel like punishment. It can feel like freedom.

Foundation: 5 minutes of anything that isn't sitting
Build: 20 minutes of something fun (bike ride, hike, swim, basketball, yard work)
Momentum: 30+ minutes of an activity you love that gets your heart rate up

Post: Photo or video of what you chose. Caption: "Joy movement 🎉"
