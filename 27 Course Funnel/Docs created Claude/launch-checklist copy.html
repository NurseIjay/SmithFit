Task 3: Configure Funnel Page Domains & SSL
P1 VA

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Go to Settings → Domains in GHL
  2. Click Add Domain and enter your custom domain (e.g., courses.smithfit.com)
  3. Copy the CNAME record provided by GHL
  4. Log in to your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, etc.)
  5. Add a CNAME record pointing your subdomain to the GHL value
  6. Back in GHL, click Verify Domain — wait up to 24 hours for DNS propagation
  7. Once verified, enable SSL by toggling the SSL switch ON
  8. Assign the domain to your master funnel under Sites → Funnels → Funnel Settings

Common Mistakes:

  • Not waiting for DNS propagation (can take 24-48 hours)
  • Adding an A record instead of CNAME
  • Forgetting to enable SSL — pages will show 'Not Secure' warning
  • Not assigning the domain to the specific funnel after verification

Verification Checklist:

  • Domain shows 'Verified' status in GHL Settings → Domains
  • SSL certificate is active (green padlock in browser)
  • Funnel pages load on the custom domain without errors
  • No mixed-content warnings in browser console
