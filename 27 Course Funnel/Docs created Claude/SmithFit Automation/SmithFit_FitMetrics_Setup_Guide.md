## PART 1 — WEEKLY AUTOMATED BROADCASTS
### Set Once → Fires Forever

These 4 messages run on autopilot every week to all members tagged `[MEMBERSHIP-ACTIVE]`.

Set them up under: **FitMetrics → Broadcasts → Scheduled → Recurring**

---

### MONDAY — NEW WEEK ACTIVATION
**Send time:** Monday, 8:00 AM (member's local time)
**Audience:** All active members
**Repeat:** Every week

**Message:**
> Good morning, SmithFit FAM! New week. Your updated meal plan is live in the Nutrition tab — check it today. Your workouts have progressed in the Workouts tab. This week's live coaching call is pinned in the community group — link is there waiting for you. Let's make this week count. You've got everything you need.

---

### WEDNESDAY — WIN WEDNESDAY
**Send time:** Wednesday, 9:00 AM
**Audience:** All active members
**Repeat:** Every week

**Message:**
> It's Win Wednesday inside the SmithFit FAM! This is your reminder that your wins count — ALL of them. Scale wins, non-scale wins, shift survival wins, habit streak wins. Drop yours in the community group right now. Tag a sister who you saw crushing it this week. We're celebrating EVERYONE today. No win is too small. Go.

---

### SATURDAY — REST + REFLECT
**Send time:** Saturday, 9:00 AM
**Audience:** All active members
**Repeat:** Every week

**Message:**
> Weekend check-in. Open your app and look at your habit streaks from this week. How many days did you hit the cocktail? The steps? The breathing? Sleep log? Whatever number you see — that's your data, not your grade. No judgment. Next week we build on it. If you missed logging something this week, you can go back in the calendar and check off habits from previous days. Don't lose your streak over a missed log.

---

### SUNDAY — PREP + PREVIEW
**Send time:** Sunday, 7:00 PM
**Audience:** All active members
**Repeat:** Every week

**Message:**
> Sunday is SmithFit Prep Day. 20 minutes today will save you from 5 bad decisions this week. Quick checklist: Check your new meal plan in the app. Pick 2–3 recipes to prep. Make sure your cocktail ingredients are accessible. Look at your schedule and pick your 3 workout days. That's it. 20 minutes. The nurses who prep on Sundays hit their goals twice as fast. See you Monday.

## PART 2 — MILESTONE IN-APP DMs
### Set Once → Fires Automatically Per Member

Set up under: **FitMetrics → Automations → Milestone Rules → New Rule**

Trigger type: **Days since membership start**
Set each rule to fire **ONCE per client** (not repeating).
Add tag `[MILESTONE-DAY-X]` on fire so it never repeats.
