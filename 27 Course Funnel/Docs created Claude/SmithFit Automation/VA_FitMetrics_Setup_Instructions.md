# VA Instructions: FitMetrics Setup
## SmithFit Automated Broadcasts + Milestone Messages

**Who this is for:** VA setting up automated messages in FitMetrics
**Time to complete:** ~60–90 minutes
**What you'll be setting up:**
- 4 weekly recurring broadcast messages (fire every week to all members, forever)
- 8 milestone DMs (fire automatically on specific days per member)
- 5 habit streak messages (fire automatically based on workout/habit milestones)

**Before you start:** Log into FitMetrics and make sure you have admin access.

---

## PART 1 — WEEKLY RECURRING BROADCASTS
### These 4 messages send automatically every week to all active members. Set them up once and they run forever.

**Where to go in FitMetrics:**
> Broadcasts → Scheduled → Recurring → + New Broadcast

---

### BROADCAST 1 OF 4 — MONDAY

1. Click **+ New Broadcast** (or equivalent "create" button)
2. Set the **title/name** to: `Monday — New Week Activation`
3. Set the **send day** to: `Monday`
4. Set the **send time** to: `8:00 AM`
5. Set the **audience** to: All active members (or whatever tag means active membership)
6. Set **repeat** to: `Every week` / `Recurring`
7. Copy and paste the message below exactly as written:

---
> Good morning, SmithFit FAM! New week. Your updated meal plan is live in the Nutrition tab — check it today. Your workouts have progressed in the Workouts tab. This week's live coaching call is pinned in the community group — link is there waiting for you. Let's make this week count. You've got everything you need.
---

8. Save and activate.

---

### BROADCAST 2 OF 4 — WEDNESDAY

1. Click **+ New Broadcast**
2. **Name:** `Wednesday — Win Wednesday`
3. **Send day:** `Wednesday`
4. **Send time:** `9:00 AM`
5. **Audience:** All active members
6. **Repeat:** Every week
7. Paste this message:

---
> It's Win Wednesday inside the SmithFit FAM! This is your reminder that your wins count — ALL of them. Scale wins, non-scale wins, shift survival wins, habit streak wins. Drop yours in the community group right now. Tag a sister who you saw crushing it this week. We're celebrating EVERYONE today. No win is too small. Go.
---

8. Save and activate.

---

3. **Send day:** `Saturday`

8. Save and activate.

## PART 2 — MILESTONE DMs
### These messages fire automatically per member based on how many days they've been in the program. Each one fires once per member, never repeats.

**Where to go in FitMetrics:**
> Automations → Milestone Rules → + New Rule

**Important settings for every rule:**
- Trigger type: `Days since membership start`
- Fire: `Once per client` (NOT repeating)
- Add a tag after firing so it never repeats (see each rule below)

---

### MILESTONE 1 — DAY 7

1. Click **+ New Rule**
2. **Name:** `Day 7 — First Week Done`
3. **Trigger:** Days since membership start = `7`
4. **Fires:** Regardless of habit completion (applies to everyone)
5. **Fire once per client:** YES
6. **Add tag on fire:** `MILESTONE-DAY-7`
7. Paste this message:

---
> You made it through your first week. That's not nothing — most people don't make it past Day 3. Your SmithFit First Week badge just dropped into your achievements tab. Go check it. And then go post in the community — you earned a shoutout.
---

8. Save and activate.

## PART 3 — HABIT STREAK AUTOMATIONS
### These fire based on workout count or habit streaks — independent of how many days someone has been a member.

**Where to go in FitMetrics:**
> Automations → Habit Streak Rules (or Workout Rules)

---

### STREAK 1 — 7-Day Habit Streak

1. Click **+ New Rule**
2. **Name:** `7-Day Habit Streak`
3. **Trigger:** 7 consecutive days of any habit checked off
